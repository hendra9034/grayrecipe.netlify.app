The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Chicken Hong Kong Style (Without Batter):
  1. Get large chicken breast, cut into bite sized pieces,
  2. Prepare green bell pepper, cut into chunks,
  3. Take a white onion, sliced,
  4. Get pineapple pieces (frozen or fresh),
  5. Prepare salt
  6. Make ready Spray vegetable oil for frying
  7. Make ready For the sauce:
  8. Prepare ketchup,
  9. Make ready white wine vinegar,
  10. Get quality runny honey,
  11. Prepare premium oyster sauce,
  12. Take light soy sauce
  13. Get Garnish (optional)
  14. Get Sliced spring onion
  15. Get Serving suggestions (sides)
  16. Get Rice,
  17. Prepare Noodles
Steps to make Sweet & Sour Chicken Hong Kong Style (Without Batter):
  1. Make up the sweet and sour sauce by combining all of the sauce ingredients and giving them a good stir. Set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok until it's really hot then add in the chicken and brown it off on all sides. Once that's done add in the onion and stir fry for another two minutes, then add the pineapple and peppers.
  3. Toss everything together and continue to fry and cook through. Season with a pinch of salt. Pour over the sauce and reduce the heat a little to medium. Allow the sauce to bubble away and reduce down and thicken.
  4. Toss everything together again, then once the sauce has thickened and the ingredients nicely coated (usually takes around 4-5 minutes), remove from the heat. Serve up with your chosen side and garnish with some spring onions. Enjoy!
