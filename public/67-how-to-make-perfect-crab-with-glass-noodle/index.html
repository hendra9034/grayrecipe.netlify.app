The ingredients needed to make Crab with Glass Noodle:
  1. Take chicken stock
  2. Make ready oyster sauce
  3. Prepare soy sauce
  4. Prepare dark soy sauce
  5. Take sesame oil
  6. Take white sugar
  7. Get white and black pepper each
  8. Make ready garlic crushed
  9. Get cilantro crushed
  10. Make ready ginger
Instructions to make Crab with Glass Noodle:
  1. Mix the stocm, oyster sauce, soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sesame oil and white sugar
  2. Pour the sauce into the glass noodle and set aside
  3. Use mortar and pestle to crush the white and black peppers, garlic and cilantro leaves
  4. In a pan, saute the crushed ingredients and slices if ginger in vegetable oil
  5. Pour the noodles and crab and the sauce and turn the heat high
  6. When boiling, minimize the heat and cook for 5mins each side of the crab
