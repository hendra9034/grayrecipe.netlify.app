The ingredients needed to make Healthy Sweet & Sour Chicken with Chicken Breast:
  1. Get 160 grams Chicken breast
  2. Take 1 Salt and pepper
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Sake
  4. Make ready 1 around 2 cm Tubed ginger (or grated ginger)
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Sesame seeds
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Katakuriko
  7. Make ready 1 add to the frying pan to about 1 cm high Oil
  8. Make ready 1 small Onion
  9. Take 1 Carrot
  10. Get 1 tbsp ★Ketchup
  11. Get 1 tbsp ★Vinegar
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp ★ Soy sauce
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp ★Chicken stock granules
  14. Make ready 1 Katakuriko
Steps to make Healthy Sweet & Sour Chicken with Chicken Breast:
  1. Cut the chicken breast on the diagonal into 2 cm pieces. Season with salt and pepper and rub in the sake and ginger.
  2. Mix the sesame seeds and katakuriko and lightly coat the chicken from Step 1 with it. Slowly stir-fry the chicken in a frying pan filled with 1 cm of oil. When the chicken browns, flip it over.
  3. The chicken is done.
  4. Roughly chop the carrot into chunks.
  5. Cut the onion into 2-3 cm wedges.
  6. Add oil to the pan for stir frying (not listed) and stir fry the carrots and onions. Add the chicken from step 3 and the combined ★ seasonings.
  7. Add the katakuriko dissolved in water and stir to thicken the sauce. Done.
