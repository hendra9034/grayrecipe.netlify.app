The ingredients needed to make Chicken baked in a buttery curried apple sauce:
  1. Make ready 1.5 Tablespoons butter
  2. Get 1 onion chopped
  3. Prepare 1 apple - peeled , cored , finely chopped
  4. Prepare 1.5 Tablespoons all purpose flour
  5. Get 0.5 Tablespoon chicken masala powder
  6. Prepare 0.5 Teaspoon garam Masala
  7. Prepare 2 chicken breasts skinless , boneless , cubed
  8. Make ready 1 Cup chicken broth
  9. Get 0.5 Cup milk

Steps to make Chicken baked in a buttery curried apple sauce:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees C
  2. Saute apple and onion in butter untill tender. Add spice powders and saute 1 minute more. Add flour and continue to cook 1 minute longer. Add stock and milk; stir well.
  3. Season chicken breasts to taste and lay in a baking dish. Pour sauce mixture over the chicken and bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes untill chicken is done. Serve warm with rice or bread.
