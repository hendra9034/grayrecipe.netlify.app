The ingredients needed to make sweet and sour chicken stirfry:
  1. Get meat
  2. Take 1 tbsp grapeseed oil
  3. Get 1 packages boneless skinless chicken breasts
  4. Take 1 clove garlic
  5. Make ready 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  6. Make ready 1 bunch favorite veggies
  7. Get 1/2 cup ketchup
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup apple cidar vinegar

  10. Prepare 2 tbsp sherry wine
  11. Make ready 1 cup remaining juice from canned fruit
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup soy sauce
  13. Take 1 can mandarin oranges or diced pineapple in juice-not syrup!
  14. Prepare 2 cup rice or noodles
  15. Get 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  16. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  17. Get 1 bunch cilantro-chopped

Instructions to make sweet and sour chicken stirfry:
  1. brown diced chicken in oil
  2. when chicken is browned /add minced garlic clove and red pepper flakes. heat until fragrant/add veggies.
  3. stir in veggies and cover wok with lid on medium heat.
  4. stir together ketchup, vinegar, soy sauce, brown sugar, sherry and drained fruit juice in a 2 cup measuring cup. add to wok.
  5. continue a simmer until veggies are al dente and chicken is cooked through.
  6. add a slurry of corn starch and cold chicken broth if a thicker consistency is desired.
  7. add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. make rice or noodles according to their packaging.
  9. add fruit at last moment before serving
  10. place rice or noodles in serving dish, top with mixture, garnish with cilantro!
