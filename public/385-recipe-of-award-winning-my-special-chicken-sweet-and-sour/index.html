The ingredients needed to make My special chicken sweet and sour:
  1. Get pieces Chicken
  2. Make ready 4 big onions
  3. Make ready 1 tbsn ginger
  4. Get 1 tbsn garlic
  5. Make ready Tomato ketchup
  6. Make ready 2 tbspn Vinegar
  7. Get 1/4 cup slices on pineapples
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup capsicums (mix green+red+yellow)
  9. Get 1/4 cup coriander
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup water
  11. Make ready 4 tbspn barbecue sauce
  12. Get 2 tspn oil
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp Salt and sugar

Steps to make My special chicken sweet and sour:
  1. In a heavy pan put your chicken and add 2tbspn water and cover and let it simmer.
  2. Keep on checking when it cooked and water starts to dry add a little oil and watch it brown as you stir.
  3. When it brown to your choice add onions, garlic, ginger and let them cook.
  4. Then add your the salt, sugar. The sugar will give it a more golden brown colour.
  5. Then add ketchup, vinegar, sauce and the pineapples.
  6. Finally add the vegetables and just stir fry as you remove.
  7. Serve, eat with accompaniment of choice.
