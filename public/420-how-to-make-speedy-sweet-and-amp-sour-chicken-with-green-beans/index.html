The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Chicken With Green Beans:
  1. Get 1 cup White rice
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup Sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup Soy sauce
  5. Make ready 1 cup White vinegar
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Vegetable oil
  7. Get 1 1/4 lb Boneless Skinless chicken breast cut into 1-inch pieces
  8. Get 2 Bell peppers
  9. Take 1 lb Green beans
  10. Make ready 5 Scallions
  11. Get 1 2-inch piece fresh ginger, peeled and minced
  12. Get 3 clove Garlic
  13. Take 2 tbsp Chopped roasted peanuts
Steps to make Sweet & Sour Chicken With Green Beans:
  1. Cook rice according to package instructions. Meanwhile, in a small bowl or measuring cup, whisk together cornstarch, sugar, soy sauce and vinegar. Set aside.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat oil over high. Add chicken, bell peppers and green beans and cook, stirring frequently, until vegetables begin to soften, about 5 minutes. Add scallions, ginger and garlic and cook, stirring frequently, until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender, about 4 minutes.
  3. Whisk soy sauce mixture, add to skillet, and cook, stirring constantly, until sauce thickens, about 3 minutes. Remove from hear. Fluff rice with a fork. To serve, spoon chicken mixture over rice and top with peanuts if desired.
