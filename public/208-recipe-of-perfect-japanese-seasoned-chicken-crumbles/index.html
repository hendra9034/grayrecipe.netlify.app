The ingredients needed to make Japanese Seasoned Chicken Crumbles:
  1. Take minced chicken
  2. Take soy sauce
  3. Take sugar
  4. Get Mirin (sweet Sake for seasoning)
  5. Make ready grated ginger
  6. Make ready Dashi powder (Japanese fish bouillon powder)
  7. Make ready Green onion
  8. Make ready Sesame
  9. Prepare Egg York
Steps to make Japanese Seasoned Chicken Crumbles:
  1. Ingredients Mirin
  2. Ingredients Dashi powder
  3. Put all the seasoning and mix well
  4. Add minced chicken and mix. Then start to cook over a gentle heat.
  5. Cook for 10-15 minutes stirring slowly with chopsticks till the chicken crumbled.
  6. Put the crumbles on rice and garnish chopped green onion, sesame and raw egg york.
  7. Mix well and eat.
