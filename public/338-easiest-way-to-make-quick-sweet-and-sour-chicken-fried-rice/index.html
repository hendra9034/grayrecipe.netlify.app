The ingredients needed to make Sweet And Sour Chicken Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 cup cooked rice
  2. Take 1 cup leftover cooked shreded chicken
  3. Prepare 1 dice onion
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp light soy sauce
  6. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Take sweet and sour chili sauce
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp ketchup
  9. Take 2 tbsp chili ketchup
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp vineger
  11. Take 1 tsp chili powder
  12. Prepare 3 1/4 tbsp water
  13. Take garnish
  14. Prepare 1 bunch red and yellow cherry tomato
  15. Get 1 bunch spring onions
Instructions to make Sweet And Sour Chicken Fried Rice:
  1. in pan bring sauce to a boil then set aside
  2. in pan brown onion with oil then add cooked chicken and rice fry for for 1minute then add sauce
  3. mix well for another minute till rice is almost dry then off heat and top garnish
