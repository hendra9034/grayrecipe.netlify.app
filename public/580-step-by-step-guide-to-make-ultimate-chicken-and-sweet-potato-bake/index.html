The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Sweet Potato Bake:
  1. Take 2 chicken breasts sliced into fillets
  2. Make ready 2 onions
  3. Get 2 sweet potatoes
  4. Take 1 small tin of tomato sauce
  5. Take 1 small bunch of fresh basil
  6. Take 1 tsp. oregano
  7. Make ready 1 tsp. olive oil
  8. Prepare Salt and pepper
Steps to make Chicken and Sweet Potato Bake:
  1. Lightly grease an oven dish with the olive oil and line it with the chicken fillets. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Thinly slice the onions and place on top
  2. Wash and peel the sweet potatoes (these were white ones but red ones are fine too). Cut long, thin strips and place on top of the onions.
  3. Add a layer of tomato sauce and sprinkle with oregano. Put in a pre-heated oven at 180ยบ for 30 minutes
  4. When ready wash and chop the fresh basil and sprinkle. Serve and enjoy :-)
