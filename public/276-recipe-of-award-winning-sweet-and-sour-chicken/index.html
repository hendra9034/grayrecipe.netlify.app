Steps to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. In a large bowl combine chicken, soy sauce, egg and 2 tablespoons of cornstarch. Marinate in fridge for 20 minutes
  2. Place remain cornstarch on bowl and toss chi chicken pieces into it
  3. Heat oil on frying pan to 350 degrees
  4. Fry chicken until golden brown; remove from oil and sat to the side
  5. Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in medium frying pan (I used a Dutch oven)
  6. Saute bell pepper and onion until tender
  7. Add garlic and ginger
  8. Add vinegar, sugar, and ketchup; bring to a boil
  9. Add pineapple chunks
  10. Add chicken and toss to coat; remove from heat
  11. Enjoy!!
