The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti squash cheesey Chicken bake:
  1. Make ready spaghetti squash
  2. Prepare 1 onion, diced fine
  3. Take 2 broccoli heads, florets
  4. Take 3 gloves garlic, minced
  5. Make ready 2 chicken breasts, cubed small
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cup milk
  7. Take 1/2 cup half half
  8. Make ready 4 tbls butter
  9. Make ready salt pepper
  10. Prepare 2 tbls flour
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup parmesan,or more
  12. Get 1/2 cup Italian blend cheese, or more

Steps to make Spaghetti squash cheesey Chicken bake:
  1. Pre heat oven 375. At this time get your squash started, microwave or oven. while waiting for squash to cook; Melt 2tbls butter, add onion and garlic.Cook about 3 minutes
  2. Add chicken and broccoli. Cook until chicken is cooked. 5 to 8 minutes. Put chicken mix aside when finished. You will be using same pan for sauce.
  3. Add 2 tbls of butter, melt, then slowly add flour. Whisk briefly.
  4. Slowly add milk and half half to pan. Whisk till it thickens. Take off heat, whisk in parmesan and salt pepper to taste.
  5. Add chicken mixture back into pan. Fold in
  6. Add spaghetti squash, fold in
  7. Pour mix into greased baking diish.
  8. Add Italian cheese on top. Bake 25 to 30 minutes. Let sit. Enjoy. You can broil a couple minutes to add some color at the end
