The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken and Rice:
  1. Make ready 2 box Chicken Rice or Roni Mix w/ Seasonings
  2. Get 2 1/2 cup boiling water
  3. Get 2 1/2 tbsp butter or margarine
  4. Prepare 1 Whole Cut Up Fryer
  5. Take 1 can Cream of Chicken Soup
  6. Take 1 can Cteam of Mushroom Soup
  7. Make ready 1 can Cream of Celery Soup
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
Steps to make Easy Chicken and Rice:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. In a 9 x 12 casserole dish pour the two boxes of rice and sprinkle with seasoning mixes
  3. Slice the butter randomly over the rice and seasonings
  4. Pour the boiling water over the rice and butter, making sure it's mixed well and spread evenly
  5. Place the chicken pieces over the rice mix
  6. Mix the three cans of soup together in a medium bowl until thoroughly mixed
  7. Spread the soups evenly over the chicken and rice to the edges of the pan
  8. Sprinkle the parmesan cheese over the top of the whole thing
  9. Cook uncovered at 350 for an hour and a half.
  10. Remove carefully and let stand for about 15 minutes as it will be very hot, serve and enjoy!
