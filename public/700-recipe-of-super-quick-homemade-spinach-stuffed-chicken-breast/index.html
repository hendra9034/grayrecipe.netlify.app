  9. Prepare Mayonnaise (1 tbsp)
  10. Take Spinach (1 cup)

Steps to make Spinach stuffed chicken breast:
  1. Preheat oven to 190 C.
  2. Use a sharp knife to cut a pocket into the side of each chicken breast. Drizzle with oil and set aside.
  3. Add the paprika, 1/2 teaspoon salt, garlic powder, and onion powder to a small bowl and stir to combine. Sprinkle evenly over both sides of the chicken.
  4. Add the cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, mayonnaise, spinach, garlic, chilli flakes and remaining ½ teaspoon of salt to a small mixing bowl and stir well.
  5. Spoon the mixture into each chicken breast.
  6. Place the chicken breasts in a baking dish. Bake, uncovered, until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Serve.
