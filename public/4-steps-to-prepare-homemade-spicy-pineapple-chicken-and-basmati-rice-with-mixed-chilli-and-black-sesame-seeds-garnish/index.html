The ingredients needed to make Spicy Pineapple Chicken and Basmati Rice with Mixed Chilli and Black Sesame seeds Garnish:
  1. Get Vegetables
  2. Get 1 Bulb Pak Choi(Sliced)
  3. Prepare 50 g Radish(diced)
  4. Get 3 Large Carrots(diced)
  5. Prepare 2 Large Chestnut Mushrooms(Sliced)
  6. Get 1 Large Red Onion(Sliced)
  7. Prepare 2 Leeks(chopped)
  8. Take 1 Large Tin Pineapple(medium cut)
  9. Get Chicken Marinade
  10. Take 4 Chicken Breast Fillets(julienned)
  11. Take 2 Garlic Cloves(minced)
  12. Prepare 1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika
  13. Take 3 Sprigs Fresh Thyme(leaves only)
  14. Prepare 1 Scotch Bonnets(chopped)
  15. Make ready 1 Tsp Chilli Flakes
  16. Take 1 Tsp Light Soy Sauce
  17. Take Stock Mix
  18. Get 1 Chicken Stick
  19. Take 3 Tbsp Corn Flour
  20. Prepare 450 ml Boiled Water
  21. Prepare Other ingredients
  22. Take 250 g Basmati Rice
Instructions to make Spicy Pineapple Chicken and Basmati Rice with Mixed Chilli and Black Sesame seeds Garnish:
  1. Prep the vegetables as stated above: lightly season and set aside. Marinate the chicken fillets(add a drizzle of oil) and set aside; room temperature.
  2. Heat up a Tbsp of oil in a sauce pan on a medium high heat. Add the marinated chicken fillets and sear for 10-12 minutes on medium heat. Add the vegetables, stir and simmer for 5 minutes. Combine the stock mix ingredients and stir in into the sauce pan. Cover and simmer on medium low heat for 15 minutes
  3. Meanwhile cook the rice (desired method). Tip: I always use the finger tip measurement for rice to water ratio. Takes about 10-13 minutes on a medium heat. Tip: use boiled water to hasten the process.
