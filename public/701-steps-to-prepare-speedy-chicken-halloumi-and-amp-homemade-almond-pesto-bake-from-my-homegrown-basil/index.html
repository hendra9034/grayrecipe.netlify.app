Instructions to make Chicken, Halloumi & homemade almond pesto bake (from my homegrown basil üåø):
  1. Before I start with the recipe please at my beautiful basil leaves üòç
  2. Put all the pesto ingredients, except the oil, in a food processor and mix for almost 30 seconds
  3. Add half the olive oil and mix for 10 seconds. Then move to a jar and pour the rest the olive oil on top of the pesto
  4. Rub the chicken breast with a bit of oil, salt and pepper and place in a baking tray
  5. Put 1 tablespoon of the pesto on each breast and top it with the halloumi cheese slices
  6. Place in the oven on 200 degrees for 20 minutes. Remove the excess of water from it then place again for another 20 minutes.
