Instructions to make Sweet and sour Chineese chicken noodles:
  1. Marinate chicken for 1/2 an hour or less in a mix of salt, pepper,paprika,ginger,garlic, vinegar and lemon.
  2. Add corn flour and flour, stir well untill all cubes coated.
  3. Fry in oil on high heat untill golden brown and cooked.
  4. Keep them a side and in the same wok stir fry garlic,onion and peppers over a medium heat.Then add soya sauce, ketchup,tomato puree.bring them to boil then put the pineapple cubes.
  5. Bring back chicken and stir well untill covered and mixed with the sauce. Add the boiled noodles and stir well.
  6. Cut the pineapple in half and scoop out the pineapple cubes.
  7. Scoop the noodles in each half and garnish with seasame
