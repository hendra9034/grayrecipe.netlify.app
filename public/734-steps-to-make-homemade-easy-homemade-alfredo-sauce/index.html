The ingredients needed to make Easy Homemade Alfredo Sauce:
  1. Get 1/4 cup butter
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup flour
  3. Get 1 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/2 tsp pepper
  5. Take 2 cups milk
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese or parmesan/romano blend
  7. Make ready 1 tsp chicken boullon, optional
Steps to make Easy Homemade Alfredo Sauce:
  1. Heat 3 to 4 qt saucepan to medium, add butter, salt, and pepper. Heat until butter is melted
  2. Add flour and whisk until smooth
  3. Slowly pour in milk and whisk until smooth
  4. Add grated cheese and whisk well to incorporate
  5. Turn up the heat to medium high and cook, stirring occasionally, until thick. You can thin with a little more milk if needed
  6. Taste. If you want, you can more grated cheese or add the chicken bouillon to pump-up the taste for a chicken-based dish. Stir well to incorporate.
