The ingredients needed to make Sweet Sour Chicken with Tartar Sauce:
  1. Make ready Chicken Thigh Fillets
  2. Take Salt & Pepper
  3. Get Plain Flour
  4. Prepare Eggs *whisked
  5. Prepare Oil for frying
  6. Get <Sweet & Sour Sauce>
  7. Make ready Soy Sauce
  8. Get Sugar
  9. Take Rice Vinegar
  10. Take Chili Flakes
  11. Make ready <Tartar Sauce>
  12. Get Onion *finely chopped
  13. Take small Pickled Gherkin or Cucumber *finely chopped
  14. Take Boiled Egg
  15. Make ready Japanese Mayonnaise
  16. Get Parsley *finely chopped
  17. Get Whole-grain Mustard
Steps to make Sweet Sour Chicken with Tartar Sauce:
  1. Make Sweet & Sour Sauce by combining all the ingredients. You can alter the amount of each ingredient to suit your taste.
  2. Make Tartar Sauce by combining all the ingredients. Coarsely chop up the Boiled Egg and mix with all other ingredients.
  3. Cut the Chicken Fillets into 3-4cm pieces and Salt & Pepper. Coat with Plain Flour, then Egg.
  4. Heat Oil, deep enough to cover chicken, to 170ºC and cook chicken until golden. Place the hot fried chicken pieces into the Sweet & Sour Sauce and marinate for a couple of minutes.
  5. Serve with Tartar Sauce on top or side.
