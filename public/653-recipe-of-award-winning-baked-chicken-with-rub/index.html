The ingredients needed to make baked chicken with rub:
  1. Take non spicy
  2. Get 12 large chicken breast skinless and boneless
  3. Take 2 tbsp granulated garlic powder
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp onion powder
  5. Get 2 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Prepare 2 tsp celery salt
  7. Get 1 tsp salt
  8. Get 2 tsp cayenne pepper powder
