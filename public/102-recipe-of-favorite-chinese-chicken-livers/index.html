The ingredients needed to make Chinese chicken livers:
  1. Prepare 400 g chicken liver
  2. Get 1/2 teaspoon corn flour
  3. Make ready 1 green chilli
  4. Make ready 2 teaspoons garlic ginger paste
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  6. Make ready 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  8. Prepare Coriander
  9. Get 1 onion

Instructions to make Chinese chicken livers:
  1. Mix the cornflour in the chicken livers with some salt and pepper
  2. Slice the onion and stir fry for a couple of minutes, then add the chicken livers and fry for another 5 minutes
  3. Then add the garlic and green chilli, fry for a couple of minutes then add all of the liquid ingredients
  4. Cook for another 5 minutes or until the livers are cooked and garnish with some coriander
