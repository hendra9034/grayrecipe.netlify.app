The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Make ready 4 chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste
  3. Prepare 1 cup corn starch
  4. Get 2 eggs, beaten
  5. Take 1/3 cup canola oil
  6. Prepare sauce
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  8. Take 4 tbsp ketchup
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup distilled vinegar
  10. Make ready 1 tsp garlic salt
  11. Take 1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. rinse and trim chicken
  2. cut into 1 inch cubes
  3. salt and pepper to taste
  4. coat with corn starch, then into the eggs
  5. heat oil in a skillet, cook chicken until browned
  6. spread chicken in a 9 x 13 pan
  7. whisk sauce until smooth, put over front and back of chicken
  8. bake at 350 for 15 minutes, flip chicken and bake for 15 minutes more
