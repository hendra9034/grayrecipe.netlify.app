The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Make ready boneless chicken breasts (cut into small cubes)
  2. Get 1/2 pineapple (diced)
  3. Take 2 red onions(diced)
  4. Make ready 1 japanese cucumber (diced)
  5. Prepare 3 tomatoes
  6. Take 2 tsp soy sauce
  7. Take 1 tsp sesame oil
  8. Make ready 2 tsp sugar
  9. Make ready pinch salt
  10. Get 1 tsp tapioca powder
  11. Get 1/2 bowl of water
  12. Take 1 1/2 tbsp ketchup
  13. Get 1 tsp mixed italian herbs
Steps to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Fry the chicken cubes until golden and set aside.
  2. Caramelize the onions in a pan or a wok. Add in the pineapples and tomatoes.Stir fry and add the herbs.
  3. Mix together soy sauce,sesame oil,ketchup,salt,sugar, tapioca powder in the 1/2 bowl of water.
  4. Pour into the pan/wok and let boil with the pineapples and tomatoes.
  5. Add the fried chicken cubes into the sauce. Lastly, add the cucumbers in. Serve with rice.
