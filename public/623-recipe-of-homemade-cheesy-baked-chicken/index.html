Instructions to make Cheesy baked chicken:
  1. Smash the rolls of rits crackers until there are no large pieces
  2. Add milk and two egss in a bowl
  3. Mix gratted cheese and crushed ritz in a seperate bowl
  4. Cute chicken into pieces or to whatever size
  5. Dip chicken in the combines milk and eggs then place and roll around in comibined crackers and cheese
  6. Pre heat oven to 350
  7. Place chicken once covered in a pan and cover with foil. Bake for 40 minutes
  8. Take off foil and bake for another 10 minutes
  9. Let cool. Add more cheese if you'd like and ENJOY!
