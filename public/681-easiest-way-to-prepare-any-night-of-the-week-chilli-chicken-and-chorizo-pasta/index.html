The ingredients needed to make Chilli chicken and chorizo pasta:
  1. Get Half a bag of pasta
  2. Take Chorizo to taste
  3. Take Mushrooms 6 sliced
  4. Take 1 tsp Garlic
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Chilli paste
  6. Make ready 1 Tin chopped tomatoes
  7. Take 1 tsp Ginger
  8. Get 400 g Pre cooked chicken
  9. Take pinch Salt
  10. Get pinch Pepper
  11. Make ready Parmesan cheese sprinkle on top
  12. Take Half onion diced as small as you can get

Instructions to make Chilli chicken and chorizo pasta:
  1. Boil pasta
  2. In another pan fry chorizo with chicken,mushrooms and onion,garlic paste,ginger paste,chilli paste
  3. When you have fried ingredients add a tin of chopped tomatoes with a squirt of tomato purée and simmer for 15 mins
  4. When pasta is cooked mix with other ingredients
  5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and enjoy
