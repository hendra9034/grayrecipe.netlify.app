The ingredients needed to make Chicken Bruschetta Bake:
  1. Get 1 can (14 oz) petite diced tomatoes undrained
  2. Prepare 1 packages (6 oz) chicken stuffing mix
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  4. Get 2 clove garlic minced
  5. Take 1 1/2 lb chicken breast cut into bite size pieaces
  6. Get 1 tsp dried basil leaves
  7. Get 1 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
  9. Prepare 2 cup mozzarella chesse shredded
  10. Prepare 3 tbsp butter melted
Instructions to make Chicken Bruschetta Bake:
  1. Heat oven to 350 ° F
  2. Lightly spray a 9 * 13 inch baking dish with cooking oil
  3. Mix tomatoes, stuffing, water, 1 cup cheese and garlic till well combined
  4. Mix chicken, basil, salt, pepper, and 1 cup cheese together place in dish
  5. Top with stuffing mixture and drizzle butter on top and bake for 45 minutes
