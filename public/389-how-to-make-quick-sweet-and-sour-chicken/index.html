Instructions to make Sweet And Sour Chicken:
  1. Rinse and trim chicken
  2. Cut chicken into 1 inch square pieces
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Dip chicken into cornstarch, then coat all the way with eggs
  5. Heat oil in large skillet
  6. cook chicken until browned
  7. Place into baking dish
  8. Sauce: whisk all ingredients until smooth
  9. Pour sauce over chicken so the sauce gets on both sides
  10. Put in oven at 350°F for 15 minutes
  11. Turn chicken, then cook another 15 minutes
