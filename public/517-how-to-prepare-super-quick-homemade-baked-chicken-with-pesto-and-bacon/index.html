The ingredients needed to make Baked Chicken with pesto and bacon:
  1. Get 4 small chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1/2 jar Sun dried tomato pesto
  3. Prepare 4-8 rashers maple bacon
Steps to make Baked Chicken with pesto and bacon:
  1. Trim the chicken breasts from any fat. Heat oven to 180c. Place baking paper on tray. Spread pesto over bacon, then wrap bacon around chicken. Use tooth picks to secure. 1. Place in oven and cook for approx 30minutes, depending on size. finish off till brown in frypan.
  2. To serve, slice diagonally and serve with either salad or potatoes and vegetable.
