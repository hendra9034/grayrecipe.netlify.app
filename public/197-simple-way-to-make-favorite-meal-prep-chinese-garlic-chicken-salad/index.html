The ingredients needed to make Meal prep - Chinese Garlic Chicken Salad:
  1. Take boneless chicken thighs
  2. Prepare eggs
  3. Take medium or 1 large sweet potatoes
  4. Prepare Spinach
  5. Get pomegranate
  6. Make ready cashew nuts
  7. Get Cornflour
  8. Prepare garlic cloves
  9. Get soy sauce
  10. Prepare fish sauce
  11. Get sesame oil
  12. Take Salt and pepper
  13. Prepare Rice wine
  14. Prepare small tin of sweet corn
  15. Prepare cherry tomatoes
Steps to make Meal prep - Chinese Garlic Chicken Salad:
  1. Peel and slice the sweet potatoes into thick slices. Fill a pot up with water and add the sweet potato and eggs and bring to a boil.
  2. Prep the chicken thighs: in a large bowl, place the chicken and add in the seasonings - soy sauce, fish sauce, sesame oil, rice wine, salt and pepper. Smash and add the garlic cloves to this. Mix and leave to marinate for 30 mins.
  3. Whilst the chicken is marinating, prepare your salad bases. Layer the sweet corn, spinach, tomatoes.
  4. Add the sweet potato and boiled egg to the salad bowls when ready.
  5. Once the chicken is ready, heat up a pan with enough oil to shallow fry. Coat each chicken thigh in corn flour before adding to the pan. Fry on a medium low heat to allow the chicken to cook whilst turning regularly. Remove and remove excess oil using kitchen towel.
  6. Let the chicken cool and then slice into pieces and add to salad bowl. Top the salad with cashew nuts.
  7. Enjoy
