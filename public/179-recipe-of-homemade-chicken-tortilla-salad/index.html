The ingredients needed to make Chicken Tortilla Salad:
  1. Prepare medium carrot
  2. Take small bunch of Napa cabbage leaves
  3. Make ready garlic
  4. Prepare root ginger
  5. Prepare Salt and pepper
  6. Make ready Honey
  7. Prepare Virgin coconut oil
  8. Get Toasted sesame oil
  9. Make ready Extra virgin olive oil
  10. Get Rice vinegar
  11. Get Soy sauce
  12. Get large flour tortilla
  13. Prepare Chicken breast
  14. Get baby cucumber
Steps to make Chicken Tortilla Salad:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius
  2. Cut the tortilla into strips, coat with extra virgin olive oil, season with salt and pepper and arrange in a single layer on a sheet pan
  3. Place the tortilla into the oven until crisp (12 minutes)
  4. Heat a grill pan on a medium heat and add virgin coconut oil
  5. Cut the chicken in half into fillets and cook until golden brown
  6. Peel the ginger and garlic and mince the two into a larger bowl
  7. Prepare the dressing with: 2 tbsp extra virgin oil, 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1/2 tbsp sesame oil, 1/2 tbsp honey, and the prepared ginger and garlic
  8. Slice the Napa cabbage into smaller pieces and grate the carrot. Slice the baby cucumber
  9. Place everything (minus the chicken and tortilla) into the large bowl and toss to mix
  10. Slice the chicken into strips once cooled
  11. Plate up by placing the salad first, followed by the tortilla strips and chicken.
