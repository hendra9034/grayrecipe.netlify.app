The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken Parmesan:
  1. Make ready 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 egg
  3. Take 3/4 cup italian seasoned bread crumbs
  4. Get 1 can (28 oz) Ragu pasta sauce
  5. Prepare 4 slice mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Easy Chicken Parmesan:
  1. preheat oven to 400°F
  2. dip thawed chicken breasts into whisked egg then in bread crumbs coating well
  3. arrange chicken in glass baking dish
  4. bake uncovered for 20 to 30 minutes until done (no longer pink in middle)
  5. pour Ragu over chicken and bake additional 10 minutes
  6. top with cheese then put it back in oven to melt the cheese. serve and enjoy!
