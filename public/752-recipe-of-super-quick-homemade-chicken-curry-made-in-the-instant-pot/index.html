The ingredients needed to make Chicken curry made in the instant pot:
  1. Make ready 1 Whole Medium chicken cut medium pieces
  2. Make ready 3 tbspn Ghee or Butter
  3. Take Whole cloves 2
  4. Make ready Whole bay leaves 2
  5. Take 3/4 Whole Black Peppercorns
  6. Make ready Cinnamon stick 1/2
  7. Get Onion 3 small chopped
  8. Make ready Chilli powder 1tsp or to taste
  9. Take Green chilli paste 3/4tsp or to taste
  10. Take Garlic ginger paste 3 tsps
  11. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Dhana Coriander powder
  12. Get 1 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  13. Get Salt 3/4 tsp or to taste
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp harder (turmeric)
  15. Prepare 1 tsp Garam masala use right at the end
  16. Get Coriander fresh to garnish
  17. Get 3/4 tin chopped tomatoes (or passata equivalent)
