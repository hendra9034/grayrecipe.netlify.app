The ingredients needed to make Wonton Soup:
  1. Get Filling ingredient
  2. Prepare chicken breast fillet (about 350 gram)
  3. Get tapioca flour
  4. Prepare oyster sauce
  5. Prepare sasame oil
  6. Take salt
  7. Prepare sugar
  8. Get white pepper
  9. Make ready garlic powder
  10. Make ready chicken powder stock (optional)
  11. Make ready egg
  12. Take Wonton pastry
  13. Make ready cabbage, chopped
  14. Take button mushroom, chopped
  15. Make ready spring onion, chopped
  16. Take ginger, chopped
  17. Get Soup
  18. Make ready water
  19. Make ready sesame oil
  20. Make ready soy sauce
  21. Make ready salt (less or more, as you desire)
  22. Prepare garlic powder
  23. Prepare white pepper
  24. Take ginger, chopped
  25. Prepare chicken stock powder (optional)
  26. Make ready ginger, chopped
  27. Make ready spring onion
Steps to make Wonton Soup:
  1. Minced chicken, and seasoning with salt, pepper, sugar, oyster sauce,sasame oil, garlic powder, chicken stock. Blend it, add egg and blend once again.
  2. Transfer chicken mixture to bowl then add chopped of cabbage, mushroom, ginger and spring onion. Mix well.
  3. Add tapioca, mix again. Filling of Wonton is ready.
  4. Put 1 spoon of filling on Wonton pastry and fold as you desire.
  5. Steam all Wonton until cooked. Set a side
  6. Now, we make the soup. Pour water to pan. Add all seasoning, wait until the soup boil. Done.
  7. It's time to serve. Put some wonton to bowl and pour the soup. Sprinkle with spring onion. Enjoy it.
