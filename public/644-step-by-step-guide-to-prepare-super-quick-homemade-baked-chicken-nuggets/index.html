Steps to make Baked chicken nuggets:
  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Cut up chicken breasts into nugget size pieces. (about 10)
  3. Season with salt and pepper or whatever you'd like
  4. Dip each piece in milk then roll in bread crumbs until completely covered.
  5. Place your breaded nuggets on a greased baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Dip in your favorite sauce! Enjoy!
