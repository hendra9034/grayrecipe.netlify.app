The ingredients needed to make Miso and sesame chicken:
  1. Make ready 2 chicken breasts
  2. Make ready Sesame oil
  3. Prepare Sesame seeds
  4. Get Marinade
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp white miso paste
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp mirin
  7. Prepare 2 tsp soy sauce
  8. Prepare Pinch chilli flakes
