The ingredients needed to make Easy Chicken Parmesan:
  1. Take 1 tbsp jar (14 oz.) spaghetti sauce
  2. Prepare 6 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
  3. Take 6 boneless, skinless breast halves
  4. Get 1 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
Steps to make Easy Chicken Parmesan:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Pour sauce into 13" x 9" baking dish.
  3. Carefully stir in 4 Tablespoon (1/4 c) Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add chicken, turn over to cook evenly on both sides with the sauce.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  6. UNCOVER.
  7. Top with Mozzarella Cheese and remaining 2 Tablespoons of Parmesan cheese.
  8. Continue baking 5 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and cheese has melted.
  9. Serve over hot pasta, if desired.
