The ingredients needed to make Baked barbeque Chicken Breast:
  1. Get 3 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  2. Make ready 4 chicken breasts
  3. Make ready 1 Tbsp Soya sauce
  4. Take 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
  5. Get 1 Tsp black pepper powder
  6. Take Pinch salt
  7. Get 1 Tbsp barbeque sauce
Steps to make Baked barbeque Chicken Breast:
  1. Marinate chicken with all the ingredients for a minimum of an hour to over night
  2. Place the marinated chicken in baking pan and brush a little oil or butter then into the oven at 200Â°c for 40 mins.
  3. After the first 20 mins brush some more oil and turn the chicken until fully cooked. Serve with some Coriander leaves and your favorite sweet and chilli sauce.
  4. Ready to serve.
