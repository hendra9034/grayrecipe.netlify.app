The ingredients needed to make Baked Chicken Mince:
  1. Take 500 g Chicken Mince *Chicken Thigh preferred but Breast is fine
  2. Prepare 1 Spring Onion *finely chopped
  3. Make ready 2 Shiitake *finely chopped
  4. Make ready *Note: You can add finely chopped other Vegetables
  5. Get 1 small piece Ginger *grated
  6. Take 1 tablespoon Miso *Red Miso OR dark colour type
  7. Take 1 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  8. Get 1 tablespoons Mirin
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon Sugar
  10. Get 1 Egg
  11. Get 1/4 cup ‘Panko’ Breadcrumbs
  12. Get Toasted Sesame Seeds for topping
Steps to make Baked Chicken Mince:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Line a baking tray with a sheet of foil and lightly oil it. *Note: If you have a 18cm square cake tin, you can use it. Oil the base and the sides.
  2. Combine Chicken Mince, Vegetables and seasonings very well. Add Egg and mix well, then add ‘Panko’ Breadcrumbs and mix well.
  3. On the prepared tray, form the mixture into a 2-3cm thick square or rectangle.
  4. Fold up the edges of the foil and form a box. Smooth the surface. *Note: If you use a tin, this process is easy. Just spread the mixture in the tin and smooth the surface.
  5. Sprinkle with Sesame Seeds quite generously.
  6. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until cooked though.
  7. Cool completely. Cut into bite-size pieces and serve. It’s a good idea to serve them on bamboo sticks.
