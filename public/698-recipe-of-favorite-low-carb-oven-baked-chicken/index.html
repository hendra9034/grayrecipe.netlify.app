The ingredients needed to make Low carb oven baked chicken:
  1. Get Boneless chicken thigh (2 pieces)
  2. Get Almond flour (1 1/2 cups)
  3. Take Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup)
  4. Prepare Cayenne pepper (1 tsp)
  5. Take Paprika (1 tsp)
  6. Take Garlic powder (1/2 tsp)
  7. Prepare Onion powder (1/2 tsp)
  8. Make ready Salt (1 tsp)
  9. Prepare Heavy cream (2 tbsp)
  10. Prepare Eggs (2 medium)

Steps to make Low carb oven baked chicken:
  1. Preheat the oven to 195 C. Line a large parchment paper on a baking tray.
  2. Whisk eggs and heavy cream in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl mix all the dry ingredients.
  4. Working with one chicken piece at a time, coat chicken first in breading bowl, then in egg wash bowl, then again in breading bowl.
  5. Place breaded chicken pieces on the prepared parchment paper. Cook in the oven for 40 mins or until it browns. (You can deep fry it or put it in an air fryer)
