The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Vegetable Noodles:
  1. Make ready Boneless chicken breasts
  2. Get Noodles
  3. Take White onions
  4. Take Spring onions
  5. Make ready mushrooms
  6. Prepare Carrots
  7. Make ready Eggs
  8. Prepare Soy sauce
  9. Make ready Sesame oil
  10. Get chili oil
  11. Make ready Chili flakes
  12. Prepare Sesame seeds
  13. Get Garlic cloves
  14. Take Thumb-sized piece ginger
Steps to make Chicken and Vegetable Noodles:
  1. Peel and chop the carrots into small pieces
  2. Fill a wok with 1.7L water, add the carrots and put on a high heat
  3. Peel and finely chop the garlic and ginger
  4. Peel and dice the white onions
  5. Rinse and chop the mushrooms and the spring onions (keeping whites and greens separate)
  6. Rinse and slice the chicken into thin strips
  7. In a small bowl, mix together: soy sauce, sesame oil, chili oil, chili flakes and spring onion whites
  8. In a separate pan, add a little oil and fry the garlic and ginger. Add the white onion and fry until brown
  9. Add both this pan and the bowl contents to the wok, stir well then add the mushrooms and stir again. Reduce hear to simmer for 5mins
  10. Add the chicken and noodles, turn the heat back up for 10mins
  11. Turn the heat off completely, add the spring onion greens. Beat the eggs in a mug then also add these to the wok, stirring constantly
  12. Serve topped with sesame seeds
