The ingredients needed to make Chicken skewers with Satay Sauce:
  1. Get Satay sauce
  2. Make ready olive oil
  3. Prepare large onion, finely chopped
  4. Take large red chilli, finely chopped
  5. Take garlic cloves, crushed
  6. Make ready medium curry powder
  7. Get heaped tbsp crunchy peanut butter
  8. Prepare water
  9. Make ready heaped caster sugar
  10. Get Juice of 1/2 large lime
  11. Prepare Salt and freshly ground pepper
  12. Take For the chicken
  13. Get chicken breasts
  14. Make ready white wine vinegar
  15. Make ready caster sugar
  16. Prepare sunflower/sesame seed oil
  17. Get little Olive oil, to fry
Instructions to make Chicken skewers with Satay Sauce:
  1. Slice the chicken into thin strips and place into mixing bowl. Add the vinegar, sugar and oil. Toss together well and leave to marinate for an hour.
  2. To make the sauce, heat oil in the frying pan under a high heat. Add the onion, chilli and garlic and fry for 1 minute. Lower heat, place lid on top and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir the curry powder and fry for 1 minute. Add the peanut butter, water, sugar and lime juice and stir over a high heat until sauce is quite thick and shiny. Season with salt and pepper. Spoon into bowl to cool completely
  4. Thread the chicken onto skewers and fry on pan with a little olive oil. I done mine in the oven for a little bit as well to ensure fully cooked.
