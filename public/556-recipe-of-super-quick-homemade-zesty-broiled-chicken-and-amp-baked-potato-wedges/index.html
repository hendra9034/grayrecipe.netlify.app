The ingredients needed to make Zesty Broiled Chicken & Baked Potato Wedges:
  1. Make ready 3 tbsp garlic powder
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp dried basil
  3. Take 1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  4. Take 3/4 tsp paprika
  5. Take 3/4 tsp salt
  6. Take 3/4 tsp pepper
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp lemon juice
  8. Get 3 tbsp olive oil
  9. Get 2 chicken breasts
  10. Make ready 4-5 potatoes, cut into wedges
Steps to make Zesty Broiled Chicken & Baked Potato Wedges:
  1. Preheat broiler on low. Line a broiler pan & spray with cooking spray. Or just spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. For seasoning mix, combine garlic powder, basil, oregano, paprika, salt, pepper. Mix well.
  3. Whisk 1 tbsp seasoning mix with 1 tbsp lemon juice & 1 tbsp olive oil. Place chicken on prepared pan. Baste with seasoning mixture.
  4. Broil chicken, turning twice, until juices run clear when pierced with a knife, about 35 min.
  5. For the potatoes, whisk together 2 tbsp seasoning mix with 2 tbsp lemon juice & 2 tbsp olive oil. Put wedges in a large bowl. Pour whisked together seasoning over wedges. Toss until all wedges are coated well. Place wedges on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  6. Bake wedges in a 400 degree oven for 35 minutes, turning over about half way through cooking.
