The ingredients needed to make Crispy Baked Chicken Tenders:
  1. Get 4 chicken breast cut into strips
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Get 1 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  4. Prepare 2 Tbsp Mayonnaise
  5. Get 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon paprika
  7. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  8. Take Vegetable oil
Steps to make Crispy Baked Chicken Tenders:
  1. Mix together bread crumbs & seasonings on a cookie sheet & drizzle with oil. cook in 400° oven for 5 minutes. This makes the bread crumbs crispier
  2. Whisk together eggs, mayonnaise, salt & pepper to make a batter
  3. Add chicken to batter and make sure they are covered completely
  4. Coat each chicken tender with bread crumbs, pressing down to ensure the crumbs stick. Place on a greased baking sheet
  5. Cook in 400° oven for 15 minutes or until chicken is done.
