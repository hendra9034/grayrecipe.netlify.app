The ingredients needed to make Quick and easy veggie pasta:
  1. Take 1 pinch dried oregano
  2. Prepare 1 pinch dried parsley
  3. Take 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  4. Take 1 medium carrot diced
  5. Get 1 small zucchini diced
  6. Prepare 1/2 packages spaghetti
  7. Take 1 cup chicken stock
  8. Prepare 1 cup pasta water
  9. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
  10. Get 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  11. Take 1 butter or olive oil

Instructions to make Quick and easy veggie pasta:
  1. boil pasta
  2. melt butter or heat olive oil in a pan, add pepper flakes, dried oregano and dried parsley.
  3. add diced carrot, cook for 10 minutes.
  4. add diced zucchini, season with salt and pepper, cook for 5 more minutes
  5. add chicken stock and let simmer until it gets thick
  6. add pasta to veggies, add pasta water and mix
  7. let it cook for 5 minutes
  8. turn off heat, add parmesan cheese. Enjoy
