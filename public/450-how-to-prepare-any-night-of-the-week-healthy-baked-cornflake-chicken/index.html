The ingredients needed to make Healthy Baked cornflake chicken:
  1. Make ready Cornflakes
  2. Make ready 4 Chicken breasts
  3. Make ready 8 oz flour
  4. Take 1 egg
Steps to make Healthy Baked cornflake chicken:
  1. Put cornflakes into a bowl and crunch half of them 1. Grab another bowl and put 4oz of flour and a egg
  2. Mix until it's stiff
  3. Add soda water or just water and a bit of sault
  4. Add 4oz of flour to another bowl
  5. Put bowls in this order first dip the chicken in the flour then In the batter and the cornflakes
  6. Put oil in the bottom of the pan if you are doing it in the oven
