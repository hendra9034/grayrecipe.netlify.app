The ingredients needed to make Creamy chicken spaghetti:
  1. Make ready 4 cups cooked Spaghetti
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup cooking cream
  3. Prepare 250 grams chicken breast
  4. Make ready 1 tbls garlic paste
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbls garlic paste for chicken marinade
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbls dried chilli flakes
  7. Take 1 tbls lemon juice
  8. Make ready 2 cups chopped broccoli
  9. Get 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  10. Get 3 tbls chopped parsley
  11. Get 1/4 cup grated parmesan
  12. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  13. Make ready 1 tbls vegetable oil

Instructions to make Creamy chicken spaghetti:
  1. Cook the spaghetti as per your preference.
  2. Clean and clean the chicken into 1 inch squares and marinate with the 1 tbls garlic paste, salt, pepper and lemon juice and allow to rest for at least 30 minutes
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a heavy bottom pan and sauté the 1/2 tbls garlic until golden brown.
  4. Add the chicken and sauté until all sides are seared. Add about 1/2 cup water to cook the chicken until cooked through
  5. Once the chicken is cooked through, add the spaghetti and stir until everything is well combined
  6. Add in the cream, parmesan, broccoli and green onions and stir until well combined
  7. Turn heat off and add the parsley and chilli flakes. Cover the pot and allow to rest before serving warm.
