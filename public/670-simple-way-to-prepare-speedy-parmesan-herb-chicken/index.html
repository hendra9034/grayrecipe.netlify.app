The ingredients needed to make Parmesan herb chicken:
  1. Get 1/2 kg chicken breast
  2. Take 2 tbls butter
  3. Get 1 tbls garlic (divided into two parts)
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
  5. Make ready 1 pc Chicken bullion (I used homemade, but the store bought is also fine)
  6. Get 1 pc scallion
  7. Make ready 1 handfull parsley
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup parmesan
  9. Get 2 tbls lemon juice
  10. Make ready to taste Salt & pepper
  11. Make ready 1 tbls vegetable oil for pan frying

Instructions to make Parmesan herb chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken in cubes and marinate with salt, pepper, garlic and lemon juice and set aside for no less than 30 minutes
  2. Heat a heavy skillet with vegetable oil and add the garlic until it turns golden brown
  3. Add the chicken with the marinade and sauté until both sides turn golden brown
  4. Dissolve the bouillon in some water and add into the chicken
  5. Cook the chicken until its cooked through, then add the butter and stir until butter melts and mixes with the chicken. Then turn heat off.
  6. Add chopped scallions, parsley and Parmesan cheese and stir to mix well and until cheese is melted
  7. Set aside and serve over a salad, in a sandwich or a wrap!
