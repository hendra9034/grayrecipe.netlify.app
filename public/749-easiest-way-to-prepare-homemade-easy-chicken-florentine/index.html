The ingredients needed to make Easy chicken florentine:
  1. Get 6 each Chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp Garlic
  3. Prepare 2 cup Fresh chopped spinach
  4. Get 3/4 cup Pepperoni or bacon-chopped
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Feta cheese
  6. Get 1 Ragu garlic parmesan sauce
  7. Make ready 1 Garlic powder
  8. Make ready 1 Season salt
  9. Get 1 Oregano
  10. Make ready 1 Salt and pepper to taste
  11. Get 1 Truffle oil
  12. Take 1 packages Fresh fettucine

Steps to make Easy chicken florentine:
  1. Saute chicken breasts. Season with garlic powder and season salt.
  2. Saute garlic, cooked bacon or pepperoni, and spinach. Cook until spinach soft.
  3. Add feta cheese. Cook until cheese melted.
  4. Stir in sauce.
  5. Cook til well blended.
  6. Fold in cooked pasta.
  7. Serve chicken over pasta.
