The ingredients needed to make stuffed cheese baked chicken:
  1. Prepare Chicken
  2. Prepare 4 chicken breast
  3. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  4. Take 4 tsp grounded black pepper
  5. Get Stuffing
  6. Take 1 dash garlic salt
  7. Take 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  8. Take 1 tsp dry thymes herbs
  9. Make ready 3 tbsp parmesan cheese (or your favourite cheese)
  10. Take 1/2 can of thinly sliced button mushrooms
  11. Prepare 1 slice of butter (amount depends on cook)

Steps to make stuffed cheese baked chicken:
  1. lay chicken breast flat down and slice it in the middle horizontally ( try to make a pocket so that stuffings can be placed inside
  2. season the outer part with pepper and salt
  3. season the inner pocket of chicken with pepper ONLY
  4. set it aside
  5. pre-heat oven to about 350°F
  6. heat up pan to melt butter
  7. add in the slices of mushrooms and teriyaki sauce (before the butter melts completely)
  8. add thymes herbs, garlic salt and mix well. Continue to cook until the cheese starts to melt (not completely)
  9. place stuffing into the pocket of the chicken breast
  10. place the chicken in a non-stick oven-proof container and bake it for about 30-40 mins or at least until the chicken is cooked
