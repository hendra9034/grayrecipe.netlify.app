The ingredients needed to make Healthy Sweet & Sour Ketchup Seasoned Chicken Breast:
  1. Take 1 Chicken breast
  2. Make ready For the Marinade:
  3. Make ready 1/2 tbsp each ※Soy sauce, sake
  4. Get 1 cm each ※Grated ginger, grated garlic
  5. Get 1 and 1/2 tablespoon ※Katakuriko
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Onion
  7. Get 1/3 medium Carrot
  8. Take 2 Green peppers
  9. Make ready 1/2 Red bell pepper
  10. Prepare 1 one clove Finely chopped garlic
  11. Prepare Seasoning Ingredients:
  12. Get 75 ml ☆Water
  13. Get 1 tsp ☆Weipa or chicken stock granules
  14. Get 1 tbsp ☆Soy sauce
  15. Take 1 tbsp ☆Sake
  16. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp ☆Vinegar
  17. Get 1 1/2 tbsp ☆Sugar
  18. Prepare 2 tbsp ☆Ketchup
  19. Make ready 1/2 tbsp ☆Katakuriko
  20. Make ready 1/2 tsp Sesame oil for the finishing touch
Instructions to make Healthy Sweet & Sour Ketchup Seasoned Chicken Breast:
  1. Remove the skin from the chicken breast and cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Put the chicken into a plastic bag along with all of the marinade ingredients except the katakuriko. Rub the bag well to rub in the marinade. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix together the ☆ seasoning ingredients and set aside. Cut the onion into wedges and the green bell peppers and red bell pepper into bite-size pieces. Thinly slice the carrots into half-moons.
  3. Add the ※ katakuriko to the chicken from Step 1 and shake the bag to coat the chicken. Boil the chicken in boiling water until cooked through. Pour into a strainer. The amount is doubled in the photo.
  4. Add the oil to a frying pan and stir fry the chopped garlic until fragrant. Then stir fry the onions and carrots over high heat.
  5. When cooked through, add the green peppers and the red pepper and quickly stir fry. Add the chicken from Step 3. Pour in the combined flavoring ingredients all at once and mix quickly.
  6. Bring to a simmer and thicken the sauce. Finally, drizzle in the sesame oil. Done.
  7. The katakuriko in the flavoring ingredients will sink to the bottom, so mix well again just before adding to the pan.
