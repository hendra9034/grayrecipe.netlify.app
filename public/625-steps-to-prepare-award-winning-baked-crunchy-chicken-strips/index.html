The ingredients needed to make Baked crunchy chicken strips:
  1. Take 1 chicken breast
  2. Take 2 cup Corn flakes
  3. Take 1 cup flour
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Take dash Six pepper

Place flake covered chicken onto pan. Add pepper to chicken for flavor. These chicken strips are much healthier and tastier than any chicken strips or chicken nuggets you'll find in the freezer aisle. They're baked at just the right temperature which gives them that crisp golden crust.

Instructions to make Baked crunchy chicken strips:
  1. Cut chicken into strips
  2. Soak in egg mix
  3. Dip egg soaked chicken into flour
  4. Crush cornflakes slightly then smash chicken into them
  5. Place flake covered chicken onto pan
  6. Add pepper to chicken for flavor
  7. Place into oven pre heated at 375 for 10 mins
  8. Flip chicken then cook for another 10 mins
  9. Remove from oven andnjoy!!
