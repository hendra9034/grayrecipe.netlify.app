Instructions to make Non-Radioactive Sweet&Sour Chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken into cubes
  2. In a bowl, mix all the marinade ingredients, mix in the chicken cubes, and let rest for 30 minutes
  3. Chop the onion and celery
  4. Mix all the sauce ingredients in a bowl with 4 tbsp. water
  5. Fry the chicken cubes with 1 1/2 tbsp. of oil for 2 minutes, until well-cooked. Set aside in a bowl
  6. With the rest of the oil, fry the garlic and ginger, add the onion and celery and stir for a minute
  7. Pour the sauce over the pan contents and bring to a boil
  8. Pour the chicken back into the pan, add the watery cornstarch and bring to a boil
  9. Enjoy!
