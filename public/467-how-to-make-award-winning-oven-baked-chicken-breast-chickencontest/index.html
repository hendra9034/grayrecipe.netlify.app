The ingredients needed to make Oven Baked Chicken Breast #Chickencontest:
  1. Prepare 4 Chicken Breasts
  2. Get 2 tspns Garlic Powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 tspn salt
  4. Make ready 1/2 tspn cayenne pepper
  5. Take 1/2 tspn paprika
  6. Prepare 3 tspns ground mustard
  7. Take 1 tbsn honey
  8. Make ready Juice from one lemon
Instructions to make Oven Baked Chicken Breast #Chickencontest:
  1. Preheat your oven at 180 degrees
  2. Slice the chicken breasts horizontally to ensure that they are about 1cm each. It should give you 8 pieces from the 4 breasts
  3. Clean the chicken breasts and pat dry with a kitchen towel
  4. Place the chicken in a bowl and add the spices, honey and lemon juice. Mix well.
  5. Cover the chicken and marinate for at least 1 hour
  6. Line your baking sheet with aluminium foil and grease the foil with about 2 drops of olive oil.
  7. Place your chicken on the baking sheet and insert in the oven, Bake for about 20 minutes.
  8. Remove the chicken. drain the liquid and return into the oven and bake for about another 20 minutes.
  9. Remove from the oven and serve with your favorite starch and vegetables. I served mine with tumeric rice and guacamole
