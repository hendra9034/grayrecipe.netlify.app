Instructions to make Sesame Oil Chicken Stew 麻油鸡:
  1. Marinate Chicken and leave it for at least 20 mins.
  2. In a pot, add 2 tbsp sesame oil and 1 tbsp olive oil. On medium heat.
  3. Add ginger slices in and cook until gingers go wrinkly.
  4. Move ginger to one side, add chicken in. Skin on the bottom, and then place the ginger on top of the chicken.
  5. Add 2 small bottles of rice wine into the pot. Cook until boiling.
  6. Use the fire gun, burn off the alcoholic elements in the soup (remember do not use extractor fan at the same time).
  7. Until the fire burns out, add half cup of water if necessary.
  8. Add more sesame oil to enhance the flavour.
  9. Finally add some cabbage into the soup and cook until they are soften.
  10. Serve with rice.
