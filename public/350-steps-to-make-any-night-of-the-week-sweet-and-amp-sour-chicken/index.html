The ingredients needed to make Sweet & sour chicken:
  1. Make ready For marinating the chicken:
  2. Take 250 gram chicken cut into cubes
  3. Get 1 tsp soya sauce
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vinegar
  5. Take to taste Salt
  6. Make ready 1 tsp chopped garlic
  7. Take 1/4 tsp white pepper
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  9. Take 1/2 beaten egg
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp cornflour
  11. Get Oil for frying
  12. Get For sauce:
  13. Get 1 tsp chopped garlic
  14. Take 1 capsicum cut into cubes
  15. Take 1 onion cut into cubes
  16. Make ready 1 spring onion
  17. Get 2 button red chilli
  18. Prepare 1 tsp lemon juice
  19. Take to taste Salt
  20. Take 1/2 tsp white pepper
  21. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  22. Get 2 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  23. Make ready 1/2 cup ketchup
  24. Take 1 tsp sugar
  25. Take 2 tbsp oil
Instructions to make Sweet & sour chicken:
  1. Place all the ingredients at kitchen counter.
  2. In the chicken add salt, black & white pepper.
  3. Then add cornflour, vinegar, egg & soya sauce.
  4. Marinate for half hour then heat oil & deep fry the chicken until light golden brown.
  5. Set a side.
  6. Meanwhile in another pan add oil chopped garlic and little bit fry then add capsicum and onion & fry until little colour change of vegetable.
  7. Now add fried chicken, button red chilli & worcestershire sauce.
  8. Add ketchup white & black pepper.
  9. Add salt sugar & lemon juice.
  10. Dissolve cornflour & add into it stir continuously then add spring onion mix well cook only for 20 seconds.
  11. Sweet & sour chicken is ready to serve.
