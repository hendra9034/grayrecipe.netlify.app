The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Get 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  2. Make ready 1 salt & pepper to taste
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup cornstarch
  4. Get 3 eggs, beaten
  5. Take 1/4 cup canola oil
  6. Make ready Sweet and Sour sauce recipe:
  7. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
  8. Make ready 4 tbsp ketchup
  9. Get 1/4 cup white vinegar
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  11. Take 1 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  12. Make ready 1 tsp garlic salt

Steps to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Cut chicken breasts into bite sized pieces and season with salt & pepper.
  3. In separate bowls, place cornstarch in one, and eggs in another.
  4. Dip chicken into egg and then the cornstarch.
  5. Heat canola oil in large skillet over med-high heat and cook chicken until browned.
  6. Place chicken in a greased 9×13 baking dish.
  7. In a medium sized bowl, combine sugar, ketchup, vinegars, soy sauce, and garlic salt.
  8. Pour over chicken and bake for 1 hour.
  9. Stir chicken every 15 minutes so that chicken is coated.
