The ingredients needed to make Sweet and sour chicken (“tang chu” chicken):
  1. Make ready (a) 300 g chicken cube
  2. Get (a) 1 tbsp soy sauce
  3. Take (a) 1 tbsp “tsuheng” wine
  4. Take (a) 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  5. Get (a) 1 tbsp salt
  6. Get (a) 1 egg
  7. Get (a) 2 cloves minced garlic
  8. Take (a) around 1 tbsp starch
  9. Make ready (b)(sauce) 4 tbsp tomato sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 1 tbsp sugar, 1/2 tbsp starch, 5 tbsp water

Instructions to make Sweet and sour chicken (“tang chu” chicken):
  1. Add all ingredients at (a), mix and leave it for at least 20 minutes
  2. Cover each chicken cube with a layer of starch
  3. Pour oil to pan, turn on heat to high heat, put chicken in, then wait until chicken is “harden”, turn the heat back to low and wait for it to turn golden, remove the chicken and put it onto a plate
  4. Mix (b) together, leave a little leftover oil from frying the chicken and pour (b) in
  5. Using low-medium heat and wait for sauce to thicken, then pour chicken in and mix
  6. Can add some white sesame seed on top (optional)
