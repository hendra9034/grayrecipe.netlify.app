The ingredients needed to make Ayam 3 Budak Gemok masam manis / 3 fat boy chicken sweet n sour:
  1. Take Things to blend
  2. Get shallots
  3. Make ready red onion
  4. Take garlic
  5. Make ready bird eyes chilli
  6. Take inch of ginger
  7. Prepare Turmeric Fried Chicken
  8. Make ready chicken
  9. Take Turmeric powder
  10. Get Salt
  11. Take Stir frying ingredients
  12. Take Curry leafs
  13. Get tamarind paste diluted with water
  14. Take cup of sweet soy sauce
  15. Make ready oyster sauce
  16. Make ready spring onion for garnish
Instructions to make Ayam 3 Budak Gemok masam manis / 3 fat boy chicken sweet n sour:
  1. Prep chicken with turmeric powder and salt
  2. Deep fry/air fry until half cooked or crispy depending on your preference
  3. Blend/pound all the ingredients from the things to blend section using blender/food processor/mortar & pestle, I prefer to have some texture in my sauce so I didn't pound it until its fine.
  4. Take out some of the oil used for frying the chicken earlier and reheat the pan
  5. Stir fry all the blended ingredients till the aroma comes out and add curry leaves
  6. Add the sweet soy sauce and oyster sauce and stir for a minute. Add the diluted tamarind paste and then stir for 5 minutes. If you cant get sweet soy sauce you can just use normal soy sauce and add 2 tablespoon of sugar.
  7. Stir again and cover the pan with lid and cook until you got your desired sauce texture.
  8. Add fried chicken and stir for awhile. Close the lid for 5 minutes.
  9. Add sliced spring onion and close the lid again for another 5 minutes and its ready to eat
