The ingredients needed to make Baked Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Make ready Chicken Coating
  2. Prepare 4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
  3. Get 1 cup Cornstarch
  4. Make ready 2 Beaten Eggs
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup Canola or Vegetable Oil
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Pepper
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  8. Make ready Sweet & Sour Sauce
  9. Prepare 3/4 cup Sugar
  10. Prepare 4 tbsp Ketchup
  11. Get 1/2 cup Distilled White Vinegar
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  13. Take 1 tsp Garlic Salt

Steps to make Baked Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven 325¬∞F.
  2. Rinse chicken breasts in water, then cut into cubes.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Dip chicken into cornstarch to coat then dip into eggs.
  5. Heat oil in large skillet and cook chicken until browned but not cooked through.
  6. Place chicken in 9x13 inch greased baking dish.
  7. Mix all sweet and sour sauce ingredients in bowl with whisk then pour evenly over chicken.
  8. Bake 1 hour and during baking process, turn chicken every 15 mins.
