The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Prepare 1 tsp garlic salt
  2. Take 1/2 tsp pepper
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Make ready 6 chicken breast
  5. Get sauce
  6. Take 3/4 cup sugar
  7. Get 1/2 cup vinegar
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup ketchup
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup chicken stock
