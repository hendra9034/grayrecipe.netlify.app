The ingredients needed to make Baked Beer Chicken:
  1. Get 12 oz Beer
  2. Get 3 Chicken Breasts
  3. Take 1 Salt
  4. Make ready 1 Pepper
  5. Get 1 Paprika
  6. Get 1 Onion powder
  7. Prepare 1 Basil
  8. Take 1 Oregano
  9. Prepare 1 Chopped dried onion
  10. Prepare 1 All spices above to taste
Steps to make Baked Beer Chicken:
  1. Spray bottom of baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  3. Place chicken breasts in dish, salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Combine spices and beer into a bowl.
  5. Use a basting brush to brush spice mixture onto chicken.
  6. Once chicken is covered, pour about 2 inches of the beer mix into the baking dish.
  7. Bake chicken for 30-35 mins or until chicken is white and juices are running clear.
  8. Enjoy! :)
