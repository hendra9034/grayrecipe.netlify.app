The ingredients needed to make Basied Chicken Feet:
  1. Take - 600gm chicken feet
  2. Make ready tpbs soya sauce
  3. Make ready tpbs dark sauce
  4. Take tps spice powder
  5. Make ready tps sesame oil
  6. Prepare tpbs oyster sauce
  7. Take Ginger (sliced)
  8. Take Garlic (chopped)
  9. Prepare - 600ml water
Instructions to make Basied Chicken Feet:
  1. Put chicken feet in pot to boil (15min) to wash off and soften the skins. Rise off and put aside
  2. Add oil to pot or pan, add in garlic and ginger to stir fry, add in chicken feet and stir fry.
  3. Add in soya sauce, dark sauce, osyter sauce, spice powder, sesame oil and stir well for 3min until the feets has the marination on it.
  4. Add in water, stir well and cover with lid to boil for 1hr - 2hr low heat under fire or transfer to slow cook pot (3hr -4hr) in between stir well (ready to served)
