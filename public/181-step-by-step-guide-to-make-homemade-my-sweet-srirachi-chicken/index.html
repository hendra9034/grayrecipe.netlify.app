The ingredients needed to make My Sweet Srirachi Chicken:
  1. Get Chicken Breasts cut into large chunks
  2. Get tsps sesame oil
  3. Get Asparagus cut into 1 inch length
  4. Prepare small Shallot chopped
  5. Make ready Green Beans
  6. Make ready Marinade
  7. Prepare Marinade
  8. Get Dark Soy sauce
  9. Make ready light Soy sauce
  10. Take Srirachi sauce
  11. Make ready Salt & Pepper
Steps to make My Sweet Srirachi Chicken:
  1. Cut the breasts of chicken
  2. Add the marinade to an ovenproof dish your going to cook in
  3. Mix
  4. Add the chicken add salt and pepper. Marinade for 1 hour
  5. Chop the Asparagus and spring onions also Shallots
  6. Add the sesame oil to the pan and fry both sides of the chicken pieces enough to colour them
  7. Add chicken pieces and shallots and asparagus keep the sauce that's left set aside and the spring onions they are for the dressing at the end.
  8. Fry these for 10 minutes so as the chicken is near cooked
  9. Should be just a little left, pour that over when adding the chicken to the oven
  10. Add the chicken mix back to the marinade cover and add to a preheated oven 170C for 20 minutes
