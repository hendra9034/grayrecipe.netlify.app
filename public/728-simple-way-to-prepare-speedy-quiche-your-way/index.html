Steps to make Quiche - your way:
  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. Grease a 2.5 quart baking dish with butter
  3. Place bread crumbs evenly in bottom
  4. Place meat evenly in bottom
  5. Chop and/or dice your veggies of choice.
  6. Wisk egg mixture and add cheese and veggies
  7. Pour in egg, veggies and half of the shredded cheese
  8. Top with remaining shredded cheese
  9. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until knife comes out clean. Enjoy your easy quiche!
