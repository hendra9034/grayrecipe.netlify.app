The ingredients needed to make Baked Chicken Egg:
  1. Prepare 1 fresh tomatoes dice
  2. Make ready 1/2 bulb onions dice
  3. Take 2 fresh pepper dice
  4. Prepare 1 tsp crushed garlic and ginger
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp veg oil
  6. Prepare 1/2 diced grilled chicken breast
  7. Prepare 3 eggs
  8. Prepare to taste salt
Instructions to make Baked Chicken Egg:
  1. Preheat oven 220degree
  2. Heat up frying pan, add veg oil toss in onion stir fry for a minute then add garlic, ginger and chicken stir fry for about 3mins then add tomatoes and pepper stir and turn off heat after 2mins
  3. Break egg in a bowl and whisk, spray cooking spray on the baking dish pour in the tomatoes sauce and finally pour the egg on top
  4. Place in the oven for 8 to 10mins bring out from the oven and serve. Bread, yam, plantain and Co will definitely work
