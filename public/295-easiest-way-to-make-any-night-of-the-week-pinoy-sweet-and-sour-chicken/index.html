The ingredients needed to make Pinoy Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Prepare boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into cubes
  2. Get soy sauce
  3. Get sherry
  4. Take cornstarch
  5. Take Oil for frying
  6. Prepare olive oil
  7. Take garlic, chopped
  8. Take green bell pepper, diced
  9. Take red bell pepper, diced
  10. Prepare pineapple chunks
  11. Get Sauce mix:
  12. Get soy sauce
  13. Make ready white vinegar
  14. Prepare sugar
  15. Prepare ketchup
  16. Prepare water
  17. Take Worcestershire sauce
  18. Get cornstarch
Instructions to make Pinoy Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Marinate the chicken pieces in soy sauce and sherry for 30 minutes
  2. Dip each piece in cornstarch and fry over medium heat until golden brown. Drain on paper towels.
  3. In a bowl, mix ingredients for sauce. Set aside
  4. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet. Saute garlic until light brown.
  5. Add peppers and sauce mix
  6. Bring to a boil
  7. Add chicken pieces and pineapples stirring well to lightly coat the chicken with the sauce.
  8. Transfer to a plate and serve immediately. Recommend having it with rice.
