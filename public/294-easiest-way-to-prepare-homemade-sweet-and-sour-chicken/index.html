Steps to make Sweet and sour chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken tights in small bite sized pieces. In a small bowl mix the egg whites with salt and coat the chicken in the egg white mixture.
  2. In a large bowl or deep plate, toss the marinated chicken in the cornstarch to coat it. Shake off any excess before frying.
  3. Heat 3 cups of oil and add first batch of chicken and deep fry until lightly cold. The chicken will come back later to be fried twice so don't cook too much, just enough to be cooked through. It should take about 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Transfer the chicken to a plate lined with paper towels. Repeat with the rest of the chicken.
  5. Heat the remaining 1 tbsp of oil in the skillet over medium-high heat. When hot (about 20 seconds) add thr pineapple and the sauce and stir to coat. Let the sauce simmer for about 2 to 3 minutes until the pineapple is tender.
  6. Return the chicken to the pan and toss until well coated with the sauce.
  7. Transfer to the plate, sprinkle sesame seeds or scallions on top (optional) for garnish and serve.
