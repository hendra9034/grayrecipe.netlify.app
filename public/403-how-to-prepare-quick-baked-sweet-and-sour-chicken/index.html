The ingredients needed to make Baked sweet and sour chicken:
  1. Make ready 3 boneless chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1 cup cornstarch
  3. Prepare 2 eggs beaten
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup canola oil
  5. Prepare 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Prepare 4 tbsp ketchup
  7. Get 1/2 cup distilled white vinegar
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp soy sauce
  9. Get 1 tsp garlic salt
  10. Make ready 1 salt and pepper

Instructions to make Baked sweet and sour chicken:
  1. preheat oven to 325
  2. rinse chicken breasts and cube
  3. season with salty and pepper
  4. dip chicken into cornstarch to cost and then eggs
  5. heart your oil in large skillet and brown chicken
  6. place chicken in 9x13 greased making dish
  7. mix sweet and sour ingredients in bowl with whisk and put evenly over chicken
  8. bake 1 hour turning every 15 minutes
