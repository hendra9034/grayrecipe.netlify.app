The ingredients needed to make zesty chicken bake:
  1. Get 6 Boneless skinless chicken breast or thighs
  2. Get 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Italian style breadcrumbs
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper

Steps to make zesty chicken bake:
  1. Place boneless skinless chicken on a baking sheet a half inch apart
  2. Mix the mayonnaise,Parmesan cheese and the cayenne pepper together spread on top of the boneless skinless chicken
  3. Sprinkle the chicken with the bread crumb mixture
  4. Bake for 35 to 45 minutes in the oven at 375degrees
