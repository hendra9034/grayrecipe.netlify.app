The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Make ready Sweet and Sour Sauce
  2. Take 1/2 cup White Vinegar
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp Sugar
  4. Get 2 tbsp Corn Flour
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Ketchup
  6. Get Ingredients
  7. Make ready 2 each Bell pepper (different colors)
  8. Get 5 large Chicken Breast
  9. Get 1 large Red Onion
  10. Prepare 1/3 cup Corn Flour
  11. Take 1 tbsp Spices of your choice
  12. Make ready 1 Oil for frying
Instructions to make Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken breast into bite size chunks.Season it with spices of your choice and then add corn flour. Mix well and set aside.
  2. Cut the bell peppers and onion into bite size chunks and set aside.
  3. Fry the chicken pieces in deep oil and set aside.
  4. Sauté the onions and bell peppers individually and set aside
  5. Prepare the sauce by mixing all the ingredients. Make sure the corn flour is nicely mixed and there are no lumps. Add 1/4 cup of water to the mixture and mix well.
  6. Bring the sauce ingredients to a boil. Make sure you stir continously till sauce thickens.
  7. Taste the sauce. Adjust the taste according to your likings.
  8. Combine all the ingredients with the sauce and let it sit on medium-low heat for 3-5 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with fried rice or noodles. Enjoy!
