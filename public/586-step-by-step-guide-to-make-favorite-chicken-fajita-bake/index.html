The ingredients needed to make Chicken fajita bake:
  1. Prepare 5 large cooked chicken breast
  2. Take 2 cup favourite hot salsa
  3. Make ready 2 cup grated mature cheddar cheese
  4. Prepare 1 envelope smoked fajita seasoning
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 cup sour cream (optional)
  6. Get 8 flour tortilla wraps

Steps to make Chicken fajita bake:
  1. Pre heat oven to180℃/350℉.
  2. Slice cooked chicken into strips and place in a mixing bowl.
  3. Add fajita seasoning, one cup of salsa, one cup of cheese and 1/2 cup of sour cream mix well.
  4. In a baking dish add 1/2 cup of salsa, spread it out then take the wraps add a spoonful of the chicken mixture and roll up tightly.
  5. Place tortilla wraps into an even layer into the baking dish.
  6. Top with remaining 1/2 cup of salsa, cheese and bake covered for 20 minutes then remove cover and finish cooking for 5-10 minutes until piping hot and serve with a salad
