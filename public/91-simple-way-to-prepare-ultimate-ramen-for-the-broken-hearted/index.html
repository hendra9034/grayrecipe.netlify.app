Instructions to make Ramen for the broken-hearted:
  1. Fry the garlic and ginger and pack choi in sesame oil for 2 mins
  2. Add the soy sauce and mirin wine. Bubble for a moment.
  3. Add the chicken stock and dried mushrooms and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, soft boil the eggs for 7 minutes and dunk them in cold water at the end. Peel and slice.
  5. Fry the broccoli and beans and prawn dumplings. Add coconut and lemon and soy sauce.
  6. Add the noodles and fresh mushrooms to the ramen and plate it all up!
