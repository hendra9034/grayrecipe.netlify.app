The ingredients needed to make Sweet n sour chicken:
  1. Get For chicken
  2. Take 1 cup breast fillet (thin sliced)
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger paste
  4. Get 2-3 pods chopped garlic
  5. Take 1 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Take 1 tbsp vinegar
  7. Take 3 tbsp corn starch
  8. Take 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  9. Prepare 2 sticks spring onions (finely chopped)
  10. Get 1 onion sliced
  11. Prepare Bunch peanuts (optional)
  12. Prepare For noodles
  13. Get 1 packet ching’s hakka noodles
  14. Get 2 eggs scrambled
  15. Make ready 2 green chillies (finely chopped)
