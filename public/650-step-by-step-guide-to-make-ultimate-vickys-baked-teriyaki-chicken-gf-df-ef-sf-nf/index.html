The ingredients needed to make Vickys Baked Teriyaki Chicken GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Take 4 boneless chicken breasts
  2. Get 120 grams rice of choice to serve
  3. Make ready 4 spring onions / scallions, sliced
  4. Make ready 1 handful sesame seeds
  5. Get Marinade
  6. Make ready 60 ml tomato ketchup
  7. Get 3 1/2 tbsp Vickys Soy-Free Soy Sauce from my profile
  8. Make ready 2 1/2 tbsp rice vinegar
  9. Prepare 2 1/2 tsp garlic, finely chopped
  10. Take 2 tsp ginger, finely chopped
  11. Take 3/4 tbsp Vickys Sunflower Butter from my profile or smooth peanut butter
  12. Make ready 2 3/4 tsp sesame oil
  13. Take 1/2 tbsp treacle/molasses
  14. Take 1/2 tsp sweet thai chilli sauce
Instructions to make Vickys Baked Teriyaki Chicken GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Mix the marinade ingredients together in a rectangular dish
  2. Coat both sides of each piece of chicken in the marinade, cover and refrigerate for 6 - 8 hours
  3. Remove from the fridge 20 minutes before cooking while you preheat the oven to gas 6 / 200C / 400°F
  4. Put the chicken onto a lined baking tray and bake for 25 - 30 minutes until cooked through. Insert a knife into the thickest part of the breast and if the juices run clear it's done
  5. While the chicken is baking put the rice on to cook as per package instructions and toast the sesame seeds in a dry frying pan or under the grill briefly until golden
  6. Serve the chicken over the rice with some sliced spring onion and toasted sesame seeds sprinkled over the top
