The ingredients needed to make Baked Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 chicken breast (depending on the number of servings)
  2. Make ready salt
  3. Make ready Oregano
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoons Olive oil
  5. Take Freshly squeezed lemon juice (optional)
Steps to make Baked Chicken:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Add the olive oil to a metal tray that is used for ovens.
  3. Add the chicken breast to the olive oil, then add the salt and oregano. (And a little bit of lemon juice if you want).
  4. Bake for about 15 minutes until golden. (might need more time, depending on the oven).
  5. Serve and enjoy😊
