Instructions to make Mike's EZ Hot BBQ Chicken Hoagies & EZ Baked Beans:
  1. Julianne half of your bell peppers and onions for chicken. Chop the other half for your baked beans.
  2. 2 pounds chicken breasts pictured. Trim any fat.
  3. Chop your chicken into 1" cubes. Add your vegetables to your chicken in a disposable pan. Add enough Sweet Baby Rays to fully coat all chicken and vegetables. Cover tightly with tin foil.
  4. One of the better BBQ sauces out there but, feel free to use your favorite brand.
  5. For the baked beans. Chop your vegetables.
  6. Mix chopped vegetables with your baked beans.
  7. Preheat oven to 350° Bake chicken covered and beans uncovered for 40 minutes. Stir beans once while baking.
  8. Serve hot. Enjoy your easy BBQ baked beans!
  9. Add your chicken and vegetables to your fresh Hoagie rolls. Add additional BBQ sauce as well. Also, add your pickles. Serve hot. Enjoy!
