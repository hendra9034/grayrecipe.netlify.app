The ingredients needed to make My Mustard Chicken, carrots and Tomato Bake ðŸ˜ƒ:
  1. Prepare 1 tsp olive oil
  2. Take 1/2 small onion chopped
  3. Get 2 Cloves Garllic crushed
  4. Get 2 Chicken Breasts cut into mouth size pieces
  5. Take 1/4 Cup white wine
  6. Prepare 2 Carrots sliced
  7. Make ready 3 tbls Cannelini beans
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbls Wholegrain Mustard
  9. Take 1 tbls Dijon Mustard
  10. Get 1/4 tsp Cracked Black Pepper
Instructions to make My Mustard Chicken, carrots and Tomato Bake ðŸ˜ƒ:
  1. Add oil to a pan heat up add Garlic and onions cook for 1 minute.
