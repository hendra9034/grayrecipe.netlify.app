The ingredients needed to make Quick easy & tasty Chicken teriyaki/sweet & sour chicken:
  1. Get 1/2 kg chicken boneless cubes
  2. Take 3 tbsp cornflar
  3. Take 1 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 tsp paprika powder
  5. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  6. Make ready 1/2 red capsicum cut cubes
  7. Prepare 1/2 green capsicum cut cubes
  8. Get 2 tbsp garlic chopped
  9. Get 1 tbsp ginger chopped
  10. Get 2 red dry chili
  11. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  12. Take 2 tbs brown sugar
  13. Make ready 3 tsp honey
  14. Get 2 tbsp vinegar
  15. Take 1/3 cup soya sauce
  16. Prepare 2 cup water
  17. Take 1 tbsp cornflar mix water
  18. Get 4 Tbsp sesame oil
  19. Get 2 tbsp rosted sesame seeds
  20. Make ready 3 tbsp chopped spring onion
