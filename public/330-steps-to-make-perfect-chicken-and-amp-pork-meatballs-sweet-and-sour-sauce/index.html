The ingredients needed to make Chicken & Pork Meatballs! Sweet and Sour Sauce:
  1. Get Meatballs
  2. Take 150 grams Ground chicken breast
  3. Prepare 150 grams Ground pork
  4. Take 1/2 Onion (chopped)
  5. Take 3 heaping tablespoons Panko
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp each Soy sauce, mirin, katakuriko
  7. Get Sweet and Sour Sauce
  8. Take 3 tbsp each Ketchup, sake, mirin
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp each Vinegar, Sesame oil
  10. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Sugar
  11. Get 1/2 a teaspoon Doubanjiang
  12. Take 1/2 tbsp Katakuriko to thicken the sauce

Instructions to make Chicken & Pork Meatballs! Sweet and Sour Sauce:
  1. Combine the ingredients for the meatballs and knead well.
  2. Shape into small round patties, and brown both sides in pan. Covering with a lid helps steam cook the meatballs evenly.
  3. Combine and mix the ingredients for the sauce (including the katakuriko).
  4. Turn off the heat, and pour the prepared sauce. Turn on the heat again and gently shake the pan to coat the meatballs. For a thick, strong-flavored sauce, simmer well.
