Instructions to make Sweet and sour stewed chicken wings:
  1. Put the chicken wings in a pan and heat slowly for the wings to release their oil
  2. Add salt and stir to prevent the wings from sticking to the pan
  3. Cook until brown then add the onions
  4. Sautee the onions with the chicken and add chilli, rosemary and parsley and stir
  5. Add sweet white wine and cover to allow all ingredients to sip into the meat
  6. Add tomato paste and cook to a thick sauce.
  7. Serve hot with accompaniments such as boiled rice.
