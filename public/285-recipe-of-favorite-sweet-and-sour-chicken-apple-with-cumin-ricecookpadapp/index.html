Steps to make Sweet and Sour Chicken Apple with Cumin Rice#CookpadApp:
  1. Place the egg in a bowl,add salt and pepper.
  2. Add water and salt to the rice and mix well.
  3. Mix the all purpose flour and corn flour together.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pot.
  5. Dip each piece of chicken into the egg,then roll into the flour mixture.
  6. Fry chicken until golden brown.
  7. Heat 2 tsp oil in a pan Over medium heat.
  8. Add garlic, capsicum,Carrot and onion to the pan and season with salt and pepper.
  9. Vegetables are just tender.stir inthe apple.
  10. In a small bowl,whisk together the sugar,vinegar,soya sauce,ketchup,red chilli flakes and corn flour.
  11. Pour the sauce into the pan with the capsicum,onion,carrot and apple and bring to simmer. cook until sauce has thickened.
  12. Add the fried chicken and Green onion and toss.
  13. For Rice
  14. Heat ghee in a pot and add whole spices and cumin to it.
  15. Once the cumin seeds start spluttering add soaked rice.
  16. Bring a rice to a quick boil,then reduce the heat to a simmer.
  17. Cover and cook till all water has evaporated and the rice is cooked.
