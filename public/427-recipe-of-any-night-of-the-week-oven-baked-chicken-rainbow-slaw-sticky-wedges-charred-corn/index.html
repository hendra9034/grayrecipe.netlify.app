Instructions to make Oven baked chicken, rainbow slaw, sticky wedges, charred corn:
  1. Rub chicken with seasoning, garlic, spices and oil and corn flour til coated
  2. Add potatoes to pan, salt and pepper and olive oil and corn meal
  3. Add chicken to bottom of oven and chips to middle, 20 mins at 190 C
  4. 2 -3 tables flour, add a little water to make a paste, use to stick breadcrumbs to chicken. return to tray back in oven for 40 mins
  5. Mix slaw with dressing made of 3 tables yoghurt, s&p, good teaspoon mustard, 1-2 tsp ww vinegar, splash olive oil. add small handful of fresh mint or parsley
  6. Put corn to char on griddle
  7. Add lemon peel to jug w table spoon fine sugar, squeeze in juice and add in whole lemon. any mint? top with ice and fill w water. mix
