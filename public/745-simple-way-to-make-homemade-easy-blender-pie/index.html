The ingredients needed to make Easy Blender Pie:
  1. Get Dough
  2. Prepare 2 medium Eggs
  3. Prepare 1 clove Garlic (smashed or finely cut)
  4. Take 1 cup Milk (you may may add more for consistecy)
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup Sunflower Oil (or any of your preference)
  6. Prepare 1 cup Whole Wheat Flower (or regular flower, as you wish)
  7. Take 2 tsp Baking Powder
  8. Prepare 1 dash Oregano (Optional)
  9. Take 2 round slices of Onion (optional)
  10. Make ready 1 dash Salt
  11. Prepare Filling
  12. Make ready 2 can Tuna (or 2 cup cooked Chicken or Turkey)
  13. Take 1 Onion cut in round slices
  14. Take 1 cup Olives, sliced
  15. Take 2 boiled eggs, cut in round slices
  16. Prepare 2 Tomatoes, cut in round slices
  17. Get 1 Oregano or Basil
  18. Prepare 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  19. Make ready 1 dash Salt (careful not to add too much as Usually the tuna comes with salt)
Instructions to make Easy Blender Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350?F or 180C
  2. Filling: Prepare the feeling first and the dough is really really quick!
  3. Mix together all Filling ingredients besides the eggs and the Parmesan: tuna, olives, onion, tomatoes, origano and salt. Mix it as if you are preparing a salad.
  4. Another great way of making the filling is using pre-cooked chicken. But tuna in a can is the fastest one and need no additional preparation.
  5. Dough: Start by blending together the eggs, onion, oregano, garlic and salt. No need to stop the blender.
  6. Add the milk
  7. Add the flour little by little.
  8. Add the oil and keep blending until you have a smooth dough.
  9. Add the baking powder, and stop the blender.
  10. Grease a baking dish with butter and flour. Add half the dough and let it spread evenly.
  11. Add the filling on top of the dough, then the other half if the dough, place the boiled eggs slices on top and the parmesan cheese.
  12. Bake from 30 to 45 minutes. It will depend on your oven.
