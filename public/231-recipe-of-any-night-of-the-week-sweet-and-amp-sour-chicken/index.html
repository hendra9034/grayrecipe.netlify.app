The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour chicken:
  1. Get Chicken (cubes & boneless)
  2. Get Salt
  3. Prepare Black pepper
  4. Get Corn flour (2 tbsp)
  5. Get Plain flour (3 tbsp)
  6. Make ready Egg (whipped separately)
  7. Prepare Garlic (1 tsp)
  8. Take Green pepper (cut in petals)
  9. Get Lemon
  10. Make ready Soy suace (1 tbsp)
  11. Get Ketchup (4 tbsp)
  12. Make ready Sugar (3 tbsp)
Instructions to make Sweet & Sour chicken:
  1. Season chicken with salt & black pepper. Then add them to coat in the two flours. Whip an egg on the side.
  2. Deep fry the chicken by dipping them from the flour to the egg and then fry till golden brown
  3. On a pan add oil and garlic then cook. Add green pepper and cook. Add lemon, soy, ketchup, sugar and cook. Then add chicken pieces and mix and cook till all sauce dries in the chicken
