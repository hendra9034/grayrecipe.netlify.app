The ingredients needed to make Easy Mac & Cheese Veggie Chicken Casserole:
  1. Get 4 tbsp Country Crock Spread, melted & divided.
  2. Prepare 1 cup Whole or 2% milk
  3. Get 8 oz Rotini pasta, cooked & drained.
  4. Take 2 cup Diced cooked chicken.
  5. Take 4 cup Frozen vegetables, thawed.
  6. Prepare 2 1/4 cup Shredded cheddar cheese.
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Dry bread crumbs.
  8. Take 1 tbsp Grated parmesan cheese.
Steps to make Easy Mac & Cheese Veggie Chicken Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°.
  2. Combine 3tbsp. of country crock with the milk, pasta, chicken, veggies and cheddar cheese in an 8-inch baking dish.
  3. Combine bread crumbs, parmesan and remaining country crock in a small bowl; sprinkle on top.
  4. Bake 30 minutes or until heated through.
