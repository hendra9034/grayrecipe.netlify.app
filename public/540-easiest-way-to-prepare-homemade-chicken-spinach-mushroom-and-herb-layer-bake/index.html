The ingredients needed to make Chicken spinach mushroom and herb layer bake:
  1. Prepare 9-10 thinly sliced chicken breast boneless skinless
  2. Prepare 1 container low fat Alouette Herb & garlic spread
  3. Prepare 1 cup chicken stock
  4. Make ready 1 bag baby spinach
  5. Prepare 1/2 yellow onion
  6. Take 4 ounces chopped mushroom
  7. Make ready 1 serving of baked ritz crackers
  8. Take 15 g Parmesan shavings
  9. Prepare 6 slices bacon cooked and chopped in crumbles

Steps to make Chicken spinach mushroom and herb layer bake:
  1. Start by placing chopped onion and mushrooms in a skillet
  2. When they begin to soften add in a whole container of herb & garlic cheese spread
  3. Add in 1 cup of chicken broth stir ingredients well
  4. Place a whole bag of baby spinach in and let it wilt into sauce
  5. Add a little Herb of Provence and pepper
  6. Start with a sprayed casserole dish layer thinly sliced chicken brest
  7. Top with sauce and veggie mixture
  8. Add a layer of bacon pieces save enough for other layers
  9. Repeat layers till you get to top. I got 3 layers
  10. Crush up a serving of baked ritz crackers
  11. Place crushed crackers on top
  12. Measure 3 serving of cheese this was 5g a serving bing I used 3
  13. Top with cheese spread out evenly. Place in a 375 degree oven for 45-60 minutes
