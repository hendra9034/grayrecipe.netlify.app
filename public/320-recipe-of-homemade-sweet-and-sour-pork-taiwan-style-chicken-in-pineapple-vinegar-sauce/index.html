The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Pork Taiwan Style Chicken in Pineapple Vinegar Sauce:
  1. Get 2 Chicken thighs
  2. Make ready 1/8 Pineapple
  3. Prepare 1/2 Carrot
  4. Make ready 1 Bell peppers (a half-and-half combination of red and yellow)
  5. Prepare 2 Green peppers
  6. Get 1/2 Onion
  7. Take 4 Shiitake mushrooms
  8. Get 1/2 shoot Cooked bamboo shoots
  9. Take For the sweet vinegar sauce:
  10. Make ready 100 ml ★ Sugar
  11. Take 50 ml ★ Soy sauce
  12. Get 50 ml ★ Vinegar
  13. Make ready 1 tsp ★ Chicken stock granules
  14. Prepare 300 ml ★ Water
  15. Make ready 1 Katakuriko slurry
  16. Take 1 dash Sesame oil
  17. Get Chicken seasoning
  18. Get 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  19. Take 1 tbsp Cooking sake
  20. Get 2 tbsp Katakuriko
Steps to make Sweet and Sour Pork Taiwan Style Chicken in Pineapple Vinegar Sauce:
  1. Chop the chicken into bite-sized pieces, season, then let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Chop the vegetables into bite-sized pieces. Microwave the carrots for 3 minutes or parboil for 5 minutes until tender.
  3. To make the sweet and sour sauce put the ★ ingredients in a sauce pan, bring to a boil, then turn off the heat.
  4. Pour about 5 tablespoons oil in a frying pan, and slowly fry the chicken from Step 1 on medium heat until crispy. When golden, transfer to a dish and set aside.
  5. With the remaining oil in the frying pan, sauté the vegetables (except for the carrot and pineapple).
  6. When the vegetables from Step 5 become tender, add the carrots and pineapple and sauté.
  7. Add the sweet and sour sauce from Step 3, then when it boils, carefully blend in the katakuriko slurry to thicken.
  8. Add the chicken from Step 4, coat it in the sauce, drizzle in the sesame oil, toss, then it's ready to serve.
