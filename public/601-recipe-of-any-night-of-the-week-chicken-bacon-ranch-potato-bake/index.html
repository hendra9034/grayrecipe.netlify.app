The ingredients needed to make Chicken Bacon Ranch Potato Bake:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  3. Make ready 1 ranch dressing mix (dry powder)
  4. Prepare 14 medium potatoes, cubed
  5. Get 3 chicken breast, cubed
  6. Take 2 1/2 cup shredded cheese
  7. Take 1 cup crumbled bacon
  8. Make ready 1 cup green onions, chopped
  9. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper
Instructions to make Chicken Bacon Ranch Potato Bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 500°F.
  2. Spray 9×12" baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. In a large bowl combine oil, salt, pepper and ranch dressing mix.
  4. Add the potatoes to the oil mixture and toss well. Spoon potatoes into a large baking dish. Leave as much oil mixture behind as possible.
  5. Roast potatoes until crispy, about 1 hr. Stirring every 15 minutes.
  6. Cut raw chicken into bite size pieces. Add to oil mixture and toss well.
  7. In a separate bowl mix the cheese, bacon and onion.
  8. When potatoes are crispy, remove from oven. Lower temperature to 400°F.
  9. Top potatoes with raw chicken & cheese mixture. Bake for another 15-20.
  10. Allow to cool for 10 minutes. Serve with sour cream or ranch dressing and enjoy :)
