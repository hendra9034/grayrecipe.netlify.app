The ingredients needed to make Chicken mushroom spinach crepes:
  1. Get Crepes:
  2. Get 1 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 2 large eggs
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup whole milk
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  6. Get 1 tsp kosher salt
  7. Make ready 1 tsp chopped fresh dill plus more for garnish
  8. Make ready Cooking spray
  9. Get Filling and sauce:
  10. Make ready 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  11. Get 1 pound sliced Swiss mushroom
  12. Take 2 garlic cloves minced
  13. Get 2 cups heavy cream
  14. Take 1 1/2 tsp garlic salt
  15. Take 3/4 tsp ground white pepper
  16. Take 115 g parmesan cheese grated (about 1 cup)
  17. Prepare 2 cups baby spinach
  18. Take 2 cups shredded cooked chicken breast

Steps to make Chicken mushroom spinach crepes:
  1. To make the crepes: - - In a blender, combine the flour, egg, milk, 1/2 cup water, melted butter and salt. Blend until completely smooth, scraping down the sides as needed about 1 minutes 1. Pour into a large liquid measuring cup and add the dill, stirring to combine
  2. Heat a 10 inch nonstick skillet over medium heat and spray with cooking spray. Add 1/4 cup batter and tilt the pan to make sure the mixture evenly and lightly coast the skillet. Cook 1 to 2 minutes until crepe is lightly browned. Flip to cook the other side, about 1 minutes. 1. Set the crepes aside to roll with filling or fold then into quarters and to with sauce. The crepe can be made ahead up to 2 hours, covered and refrigerate.
  3. To make the filling and sauce: - - In a large skillet, heat the butter over medium high heat. Add the mushroom and cook in one layer, without disturbing until browned about 4 minutes. Flip and continue cooking until tender and browned, stirring occasionally about 5 minutes 1. Add the garlic and stir constantly until fragrant about 1 minute. Stir in the cream, garlic salt and white pepper and simmer, stirring occasionally to check the consistency until thickened 5 - 8 minutes. - Add the parmesan and stir until smooth and melted. 1. Measure out 3/4 cup of the mushroom sauce and set aside in a small bowl. Add the spinach to the sauce remaining in the pan to cook, stirring often until spinach is wilted approximately 2 minutes.
  4. Fold in the chicken and cook until heated through about 1 minute. 1. To filling the crepes, spoon 1/4 cup chicken mixture onto each crepe and roll up. - Serve filled and folded crepes immediately, topped with reserved mushroom sauce and chopped dill. Enjoy
