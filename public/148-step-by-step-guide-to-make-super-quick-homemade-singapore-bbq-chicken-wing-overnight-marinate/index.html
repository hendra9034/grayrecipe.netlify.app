The ingredients needed to make Singapore BBQ Chicken Wing (overnight marinate):
  1. Take garlic
  2. Get ginger
  3. Prepare shallots
  4. Take sesame oil
  5. Prepare soy sauce
  6. Prepare oyster sauce
  7. Get fish sauce
  8. Get honey
  9. Make ready salt
  10. Take white pepper
  11. Prepare chicken wings
  12. Get Optional 1/2 teaspoon of chilli pepper flakes
  13. Get Optional 1 lime to serve
Instructions to make Singapore BBQ Chicken Wing (overnight marinate):
  1. Garlic, ginger, shallots and sesame oil in a blender to make a paste
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients except lime and marinade the chicken wings overnight
  3. Oven or charcoal grill your chicken wings. Serve with lime or garlic chilli sauce!
