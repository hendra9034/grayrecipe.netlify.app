The ingredients needed to make Quick and Easy Chicken Stir-Fry:
  1. Make ready 4 chicken breasts, cut into bitesize pieces
  2. Take 350 g frozen broccoli florets
  3. Make ready 16 medium sized chestnut mushrooms, thinly sliced
  4. Make ready 1 medium onion, peeled and diced
  5. Take 4 garlic cloves, peeled and finely diced
  6. Take 1 heaped tsp, grated ginger
  7. Get 2 tbsp olive oil
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp sesame oil
  9. Take 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
Steps to make Quick and Easy Chicken Stir-Fry:
  1. Heat the sesame oil and olive oil in a wok over medium-high.
  2. Fry the garlic, ginger and onion for 1-3 minutes (don't let it burn).
  3. Add the mushrooms and chicken and stir-fry for 5-8 minutes or until chicken is just cooked through. Remove the chicken and vegetables to set aside but leave the liquid in the pan.
  4. Add the broccoli to the wok and stir-fry until cooked through or still al dente.
  5. Return the chicken and vegetables to the wok. Add the vinegar and soy sauce to the pan. Stir-fry for a minute then serve over rice, sprinkled with chilli flakes.
