The ingredients needed to make White Mabo Dofu Japanese Style:
  1. Prepare Tofu *medium soft type such as ‘Momen Tofu’
  2. Get Pork Mince
  3. Make ready Garlic *finely chopped
  4. Get small piece Ginger *finely chopped or grated
  5. Take thick Spring Onions *finely chopped
  6. Get Oil
  7. Make ready <Sauce>
  8. Prepare Chicken Stock *OR 1 cup Water & 1 teaspoon Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  9. Make ready White Miso
  10. Prepare Sake
  11. Get Sugar
  12. Prepare Salt *alter the amount depending on the saltiness of the Miso and Chicken Stock
  13. Prepare Sesame Oil
  14. Get Potato Starch
Instructions to make White Mabo Dofu Japanese Style:
  1. Prepare vegetables. Basically you need to finely chop up everything. Cut Tofu into bite-size cubes.
  2. Place all the sauce ingredients in a mixing bowl and mix well. This has to be done before you start cooking. Cooking process doesn’t take long at all.
  3. Heat Oil in a large frying pan or a wok, cook Pork Mince and vegetables, breaking up the mince.
  4. Before you add the sauce mixture into the pan, mix it well again. Then pour it in and bring to the boil and let the sauce thicken.
  5. Add Tofu and gently mix and combine.
