The ingredients needed to make Thai sweet and sour chicken.:
  1. Prepare 1/2 tbsp cumin
  2. Take 1/2 tbsp ground coriander
  3. Take 4 chicken breasts
  4. Prepare 1/2 - full box of pineapple juice
  5. Take 2 limes
  6. Make ready 8 spring onions
  7. Prepare 2 peppers
  8. Take 2 tinned chop tomatoes
  9. Get 1 portion of chopped coriander (to your liking)
  10. Get 2 chilis
  11. Get 1 noodles or rice
  12. Get 2 tbsp cornflour

Steps to make Thai sweet and sour chicken.:
  1. Place chicken fillets in casserole dish, chop up all vegetables add to dish.
  2. mix desired amount of juice and tomatoes together with the cornflour then place in the dish make sure this is enough liquid to cover the chicken completely.
  3. Place all in preheated oven for 45 minutes for best results do all the prep the night before and allow the chicken to marinade in the juices.
  4. while in the oven make rice or noodles and serve when ready.
