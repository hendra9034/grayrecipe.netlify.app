The ingredients needed to make Sweet and sour sauce with chicken tempura:
  1. Take For the sauce:
  2. Get 18 oz orange pineapple blend juice / I use dole
  3. Make ready 2/3 cup sugar
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup white vinegar
  5. Prepare 2/3 cup ketchup
  6. Get 2 tsp ginger powder
  7. Get 3 tbsp soy sauce
  8. Prepare For the batter:
  9. Get 2 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 2 cup club soda
  11. Make ready 1 1/4 cup corn starch
  12. Prepare 1 1/4 cup flour
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp each of baking powder, onion powder and garlic powder
  14. Make ready For the chicken:
  15. Make ready 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast cut into 1" cubes. Set aside
  16. Get 3 cups vegetable oil

Steps to make Sweet and sour sauce with chicken tempura:
  1. Simmer sauce
  2. In sauce pot add all the sauce ingredients and first cook on medium for 10 minutes. Keep stirring so bottom won't burn.
  3. Then set to simmer for another 10 minutes keeping an eye on so not to burn.
  4. Take 1 tablespoon of sauce out and set aside. Take 1 tablespoon cornstarch and add to bit of sauce. Mix until smooth then add to pot of sauce. This will help thicken sauce.
  5. In separate bowl add all the batter ingredients and mix well. Let sit while you heat up a large pan of oil.
  6. Drop chicken in batter, gently shake off excess and drop into oil. Cook chicken in several batches and fry 3-4 minutes each side until golden
  7. Drain chicken on paper towels.
  8. Set out your sauce and dip away. You can serve with rice steamed with diced onions and peppers.
  9. Enjoy
