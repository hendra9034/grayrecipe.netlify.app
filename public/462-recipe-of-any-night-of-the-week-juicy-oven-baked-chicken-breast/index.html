The ingredients needed to make Juicy Oven Baked Chicken Breast:
  1. Make ready 1 tablespoon brown sugar packed
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoons paprika
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon dried oregano or thyme, parsley, rosemary
  4. Get 1 teaspoon salt or seasoned salt, Adobo or chicken salt for extra flavour)
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  7. Get 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  8. Take 1 teaspoon chili powder or 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  9. Prepare 4 large chicken breasts
  10. Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
  11. Get 3 tablespoons butter
  12. Make ready 2 cloves garlic minced
  13. Prepare 2 teaspoons fresh chopped parsley
Steps to make Juicy Oven Baked Chicken Breast:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°
  2. Using a meat mallet or rolling pin pound each chicken breast to 2cm at the thickest part. Make sure your fillets are all the same thickness to ensure even cooking.
  3. Combine sugar,paprika, oregano,salt,powders, pepper and chili
  4. Transfer chicken to the pan and toss chicken in the seasoning.Drizzle with the oil and rub seasoning all over to evenly coat.
  5. Bake chicken in preheated oven for 16-18 minutes or until internal temperature is 165°F (75°C) using a meat thermometer.
  6. While chicken is in the oven melt butter in a small skillet. Sauté garlic until fragrant (30 seconds)and remove pan from heat. Stir in parsley then pour butter mixture over chicken in oven.
  7. Broil on high heat during the last 2-3 minutes of cooking until golden and crisp.
  8. Remove pan from oven transfer chicken to serving plates and let rest for 5 minutes.
  9. To serve drizzle pan juices over the chicken and garnish with freshly chopped parsley.
