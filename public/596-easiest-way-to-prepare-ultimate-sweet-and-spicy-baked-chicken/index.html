Instructions to make Sweet and spicy baked chicken:
  1. Pre heat oven 350
  2. Put your chicken in a cooking glass pan add seasonings
  3. Add your Louisiana hot sauce and sweet bbq sauce on the chicken as much as you like for the heat and sweetness then add the garlic cloves on top if you want.
  4. Put in oven and bake on 350 for 30 to 45 minutes or until done. Make sure it's not red or pink.
  5. Enjoy.
