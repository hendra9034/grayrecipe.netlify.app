  4. Prepare 10 piece thin cut chicken breasts
  5. Take 2 tbsp Light brown sugar

Instructions to make Baked BBQ chicken with Peppers & Onions:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 475°
  2. Slice onion and pepper. Add to large bowl and combine with light brown sugar and 1/2 BBQ sauce
  3. Rinse chicken, pat dry & add to bowl with peppers, onion, & BBQ sauce
  4. Mix chicken and add half of what's left of bottle of BBQ sauce, leaving some BBQ sauce left over.
  5. Cover with foil after mixing well & refrigerate atleast 30 minutes
  6. When ready, add to pan. You can keep peppers &Onions separate to one side of pan if you choose or on top of chicken & add remaining BBQ sauce on top
  7. Bake for 25 minutes. Then let sit for about 3 minutes before serving. And Enjoy!
