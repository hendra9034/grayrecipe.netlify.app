The ingredients needed to make Sweet And Sour Chicken Balls:
  1. Take Cold Water
  2. Take Cider Vinegar
  3. Prepare Tomato Ketchup
  4. Prepare Caster Sugar
  5. Prepare Cornflour
  6. Make ready Pineapple Juice
  7. Make ready Chicken Thigh
  8. Get Eggs
  9. Get Siracha Hot Sauce
  10. Take Panko Bread Crumb
  11. Prepare Spring Onion
  12. Prepare Red Chilli
Steps to make Sweet And Sour Chicken Balls:
  1. Dice Chicken Thigh Into Bite Size Pieces.
  2. Mix Egg And Siracha Together.
  3. Roll Chicken In Flour. - Dip In Egg Mix. - Coat In Panko. - Fry. (In A Wok Is Perfect)
  4. Add The Water, Vinegar, Pineapple Juice, Ketchup And Sugar Into A Pan. - Dissolve Cornflour In 2 Tablespoons Of Water And Add To Pan. - Continuously Stirring, Bring All Ingredients To The Boil, Then Turn Down To A Simmer. - Add The Pineapple Chunks, And Stir For Another 2 Minutes.
  5. Add All The Components To A Wok/Frying Pan And Coat Chicken.
  6. Add To A Bowl And Garnish With Thinly Sliced Spring Onion And Chilli.
