The ingredients needed to make My Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta Bake:
  1. Prepare 4 large chicken breast diced
  2. Prepare Oil for pan
  3. Get 1 lb pasta
  4. Make ready 1 pack grated cheese
  5. Make ready Creamy sauce
  6. Prepare 250 ml cream
  7. Get 4 tbsp butter
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp flour
  9. Prepare 300 ml milk
  10. Take 3 tbsp cajun powder
  11. Get 1 tbsp paprika
  12. Make ready 1 tsp chilli flakes
  13. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  14. Prepare Salt and pepper to taste
  15. Make ready Roasted veg
  16. Make ready 1 onion diced
  17. Make ready 5-6 tomato diced
  18. Get 1/2 courgette diced
  19. Make ready 1 pepper diced
  20. Get 1 small garlic bulb
  21. Make ready 2 tbsp oil for roast veg
Steps to make My Creamy Cajun Chicken Pasta Bake:
  1. Chop veg season and roast for 20 mins once done squeeze out garlic from blub
  2. Chop chicken and brown all over set aside
  3. Melt butter and add flour stirring to prevent burning for 1 min
  4. Add milk stirring constantly
  5. Add 1/2 cheese,seasoning and cream cook for 5 minutes
  6. Cook pasta al-dente drain keeping 1 cup of pasta water aside
  7. Add chicken, veg and pasta mix well (if you think it's too thick add some pasta water)
  8. Put in oven proof dish top with remaining cheese for 30mins covered the uncovered for 10mins @180°c…enjoy
