The ingredients needed to make Rae's Easy Chicken Veggie Rice Bake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup instant brown rice
  2. Take 1 3/4 cup chicken broth
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup shredded chicken breast or canned chicken, drained
  4. Prepare 1/2 packages frozen mixed veggies, thawed
  5. Prepare 1 can condensed cream of celery soup
  6. Get 1/2 cup milk
  7. Take 3/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese, sharp
  8. Take 1 pinch black pepper
  9. Make ready 1 can buttermilk biscuits (10 count)
Steps to make Rae's Easy Chicken Veggie Rice Bake:
  1. preheat oven to 375°F
  2. prepare rice in microwave according to package directions, using broth instead of water(uncle Ben's said cook for 8 minutes)
  3. I thawed the veggies in the microwave too
  4. coat inside of casserole dish with nonstick spray (I used 3 qt and had plenty of room so smaller would probably work)
  5. combine all ingredients except for biscuits in casserole dish and stir to combine
  6. place biscuits evenly over the top of the chicken and rice mixture
  7. bake uncovered for 20 to 25 minutes until biscuits are golden brown
