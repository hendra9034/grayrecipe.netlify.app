Steps to make Korean Bibimbap:
  1. Marinate Meat (Let it marinate while we prepare the rest)
  2. Cool rice or use leftover
  3. Put all your sauce ingredients into a bowl and mix it around evenly
  4. Cut all vegetables into strips
  5. Rehydrate kelp, Shiitake & cut into strips
  6. Stir fry all vegetables lightly and separately
  7. Cut seasoned seaweed into stripped
  8. Set them aside separately
  9. Stir fry marinated meat
  10. Scoop rice into a bowl
  11. Put cooker assortments & meat on top of the rice
  12. Fry an egg and put it on top of everything (Sunnyside up)
  13. Pour sauce on top of everything & viola!
