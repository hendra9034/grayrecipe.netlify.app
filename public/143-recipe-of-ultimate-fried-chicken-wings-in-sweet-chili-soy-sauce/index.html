The ingredients needed to make Fried Chicken Wings in Sweet Chili Soy Sauce:
  1. Get Chicken wings
  2. Get Starch
  3. Make ready spring onion(minced)
  4. Take glove garlic(chopped)
  5. Take minced ginger
  6. Take water
  7. Take soy sauce
  8. Prepare sugar
  9. Get sesame oil
  10. Make ready sesame seed
  11. Prepare chili powder (to taste)
Steps to make Fried Chicken Wings in Sweet Chili Soy Sauce:
  1. Cut chicken wings and powder starch (any starch is ok).
  2. Fry chicken wings in vegetable oil for 4~5 minutes until the skin turn brown and crispy.
  3. Mix all sauce ingredients.
  4. Put fried chicken wings in sauce and mix well.
