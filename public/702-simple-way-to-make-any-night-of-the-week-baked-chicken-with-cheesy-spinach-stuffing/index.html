The ingredients needed to make Baked chicken with cheesy spinach stuffing:
  1. Make ready 2 Chicken breast
  2. Take Paprika powder, salt and pepper (enough to season the chicken)
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Olive oil
  4. Prepare Spinach stuffing
  5. Take 1.5 cup spinach leaves (chopped or use baby spinach)
  6. Get 80 g (4 tbsp) cream cheese
  7. Get 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  9. Get 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp onion powder
  11. Get 1/4 tsp salt

Steps to make Baked chicken with cheesy spinach stuffing:
  1. Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, paprika and olive oil.
  2. Put all ingredients for spinach stuffing in a mixing bowl and mixed well.
  3. Butterflied the chicken breast or make chicken pocket. Stuffed the chicken with spinach stuffing (just like making sandwich but with chicken instead of bread).
  4. Placed chicken on the baking tray and baked on 170-180°C for 35 mins, depending on the size of your chicken (until chicken is cooked).
  5. Served your chicken with your preferred side dish.
