The ingredients needed to make Claires Sweet n Sour Chicken:
  1. Take 3 chicken thighs cut into small pieces
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp soy sauce
  3. Take 1 tbsp mirin
  4. Make ready 4 tbsp flour mixed with 4 tablespoons cornflour
  5. Get 2 eggs whisked
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup olive oil, extra virgin for frying
  7. Take 1 Sauce
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp pineapple juice
  9. Get 3 tbsp tomatoe sauce or chutney
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp mirin
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  12. Get 4 tbsp sugar I used white but you could use brown
  13. Get 1 tbsp pineapple juice extra mixed with 2 teaspoons of cornflour
  14. Make ready 1 Vegetables
  15. Take 6 mushrooms chopped
  16. Take 1 cup pumpkin cut in matchsticks boil for a few mins first.
  17. Get 2 carrots finely chopped in matchsticks boil with the pumpkin
  18. Get 1 onion sliced
  19. Make ready 1 can pineapple slices I then cut them into small pieces.
  20. Make ready 2 peppers what ever colour you like chopped
Steps to make Claires Sweet n Sour Chicken:
  1. Marinate chicken for 3 hours if you can.
  2. Boil the pumpkin and carrot and drain.
  3. Put chicken in flour then egg and then back in flour.
  4. Make the sauce in a bowl.Keep the 1tablespoon of pineapple and the2 teaspoons of cornflour seperate at this stage.
  5. Fry chicken you may need to cook in 2 or 3 batches.Remove to drain on paper towels.
  6. Fry all the other vegetables you will need to add some more oil to pan.Add pineapple.
  7. Add sauce and stir then add cornflour and pineapple mixture.This will thicken sauce.
  8. Put chicken back in the pan.Stir the sauce it will stick to the chicken when its at the right consistency.
  9. Serve sraight away.Yummy crispy coating with moist tender meat inside.With sticky sweetn sour sauce.
