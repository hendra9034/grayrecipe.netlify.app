The ingredients needed to make Simple Shredded chicken:
  1. Take 6 chicken drumsticks
  2. Get 1 stalk spring onion
  3. Take 2 inches ginger
  4. Get Bean sprouts (optional)
  5. Get Sauce
  6. Take 1 tbsp soya sauce
  7. Get 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tsp sesame sauce
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Prepare Oil sauce
  11. Prepare 1 shallot (peel into layers)
  12. Prepare 4 garlic cloves (bruised)
  13. Make ready 4 slices gingers
  14. Get 1 stalk spring onion (white part)
  15. Take 2 tbsp oil
Steps to make Simple Shredded chicken:
  1. In a big pot add boiling water. Then add chicken, spring onion, ginger in. Sprinkle salt.
  2. Once water boiling, turn to low heat (3) and let it cook for about 30 mins.
  3. In a smaller pot, add 2 tbsp oil and when hot enough add all the sauce oil ingredients in. Stir fry it on low heat (3) for about 10 mins until all turn browny.
  4. Once chicken is cooked, remove the bone and shred them into smaller pcs.
  5. Place them on the plate and pour sauce over and then oil sauce.
  6. Add the green chopped spring onions on top when serve.
  7. You can use the left over water to blanch the bean sprouts and water as soup.
  8. Serve bean sprouts with the chicken.
