The ingredients needed to make Sweet And Sour Chicken Bacon Wraps:
  1. Make ready 1 lb Boneless, skinless chicken breast tenders
  2. Prepare 1 lb Bacon
  3. Take 1 pinch Garlic powder
  4. Get 1 pinch Chili powder
  5. Get 1 pinch Red pepper flakes
  6. Prepare 1 Salt and pepper
  7. Take 1 cup Brown sugar (May need more)
Steps to make Sweet And Sour Chicken Bacon Wraps:
  1. Pre heat oven to 400°F. Grease a cookie sheet.
  2. Sprinkle garlic, chili powder, red pepper flakes, salt and pepper on each chicken tender. Then wrap with bacon.
  3. Roll wrap in brown sugar. Then place on greased cookie sheet.
  4. Bake in oven 30-45 minutes or until crisp.
