The ingredients needed to make Cheddar baked chicken:
  1. Get 2 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 2 cups thick shredded extra sharp cheddar cheese
  3. Take 1 package taco seasoning
  4. Take 1 package mac and cheese powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
  6. Take 2 eggs

Instructions to make Cheddar baked chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Cover baking sheet with parchment paper
  3. In a bowl whisk together eggs and milk, in another bowl toss together shredded cheese, mac and cheese powder, and taco seasoning.
  4. Cut chicken in half so you have 4 pieces
  5. Dip each piece of chicken in the egg/milk mixture, then press the chicken in the cheese mixture to coat all sides.
  6. Place chicken onto parchment lined baking sheet. Bake for 40 minutes or until cooked through
