The ingredients needed to make Chicken sesame:
  1. Prepare 5 chicken wings
  2. Make ready Italian herbs
  3. Get Salt & pepper
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoon tomato sauce
  5. Make ready 2 tablespoon chili sauce
  6. Take 2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  7. Take 2 tablespoon sweet soy sauce
  8. Get as needed corn flour
  9. Prepare white sesame seeds

Instructions to make Chicken sesame:
  1. Put in italian herbs, salt & pepper in water and boil the chicken wings until well-cooked.. put aside the wings => add tomato sauce, chili sauce, oyster sauce & sweet soy sauce to the excess boiling water.. stir until mixed well in low heat
  2. Lighty cover the wings with corn flour then fry until golden brown => put the wings in the sauce, let them sit in the heat for 3-4 minutes
  3. Sprinkle the wings with sesame seeds.. Chicken wings are ready to serve ^o^ let's eat :q
