The ingredients needed to make 姜酒鸡 Ginger Rice Wine Chicken:
  1. Get Chicken Thighs (deboned, cut into bite size)
  2. Make ready Chicken Thighs Bone
  3. Make ready soya sauce
  4. Prepare to taste salt
  5. Take Gf Rice wine
  6. Take thin ginger
  7. Prepare sesame oil
Steps to make 姜酒鸡 Ginger Rice Wine Chicken:
  1. In a small pot, add 2 cup of water and boil the chicken bone to make stock. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, add sesame oil and fry the ginger slices until crispy, remove them and place them in a deep pot.
  3. In chicken on the pan, sprinkle salt on top. fry them until skin is crispy.
  4. In the pot, add cooking wine and about 1 cup of chicken stock.
  5. Add chicken into the pot, let it cook for another 20 mins on power 5.
  6. Add soya sauce in.
  7. Once chicken is cooked, reduce to heat power 2 and cover the lid for about 10 mins.
  8. Serve with rice.
