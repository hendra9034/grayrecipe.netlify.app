  2. Ab is mein chicken wings shamil kar lain.
  3. Ab is mein namak, kali mirch, chinni, soy sauce, sweet chilli sauce aur lemon juice shamil kar lain.
  4. Aur ache se mix kar lain.
  5. Ab isko medaim flam per paka lain.
  6. Yahain taq ke ache se gal jain.
  7. Pak jain to nikal lain aur ache se thanda kar lain.

  9. Aur baraf wale pani se patla paste bana lain.
  10. Ab alag se 1/2cup crispy chicken mix aur 1cup maida ko ache se mix kar lain.
