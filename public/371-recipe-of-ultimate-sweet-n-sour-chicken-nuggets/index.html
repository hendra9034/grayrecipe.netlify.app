The ingredients needed to make Sweet N Sour Chicken Nuggets:
  1. Make ready sweet n sour sauce
  2. Get 1/4 cup water
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup vinegar
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup ketchup (catsup)
  6. Take 2 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  8. Prepare topping n side
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup diced or chunk pineapple (canned)
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup canned sliced carrots
  11. Take 15 frozen chicken nuggets
  12. Get 1 any other veggie u would like to add
  13. Get 3 cup cooked rice
  14. Prepare other:
  15. Prepare 1 hot oil for frying
  16. Prepare 2 tbsp corn starch and 1/4cup cold water ( for thickening)
Instructions to make Sweet N Sour Chicken Nuggets:
  1. cook rice according to package directions. set aside.
  2. heat oil and fry nuggets until fully cooked. set aside on paper towel to drain excess grease..
  3. in a saucepan(for the sweet n sour sauce) place water, pineapple juice, vinegar and sugar. heat and stir until sugar is dissolved. add rest of sauce ingredients and heat until simmer.
  4. add pineapple and carrots to sauce. continue to simmer until fully heated. ( if u want to add other veggies do this now.. raw veggies with have to be sauteed or blanched until tender first)
  5. in a small cup mix corn starch and water. pour into sweet n sour sauce to thicken.
  6. stir continuously until thickened and heated through.
  7. place rice on a plate. arange 5 nuggets on top of the rice. then pour 1/3 of sauce all over..
  8. serve and enjoy..
