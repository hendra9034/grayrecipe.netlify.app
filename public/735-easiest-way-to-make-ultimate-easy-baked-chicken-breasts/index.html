The ingredients needed to make Easy Baked Chicken Breasts:
  1. Make ready 2 chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp 0live oil
  3. Get 1 tsp seasoned salt
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  5. Prepare 4 tbsp parmesan cheese(Kraft is fine)
  6. Prepare 1 tsp paprika
  7. Take 1 tbsp dried parsley flakes
Instructions to make Easy Baked Chicken Breasts:
  1. Spray a 9x13 baking pan.
  2. Put the chicken breast in the baking pan.
  3. Drizzle or brush chicken with olive oil and sprinkle with remaining ingredients in order of ingredients.
  4. Cook for 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and juices run clear. At 375°F.
