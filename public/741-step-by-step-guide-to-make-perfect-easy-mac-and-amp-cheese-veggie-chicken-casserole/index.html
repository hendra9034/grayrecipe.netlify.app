The ingredients needed to make Easy Mac & Cheese Veggie Chicken Casserole:
  1. Get 4 tbsp Country Crock spread melted divided
  2. Take 1 cup milk
  3. Get 8 oz rotini pasta cooked & drained
  4. Make ready 2 cup diced cooked chicken
  5. Make ready 4 cup frozen vegies, thawed
  6. Get 2 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  8. Get 1 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
Steps to make Easy Mac & Cheese Veggie Chicken Casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 400, combine 3tbs. of Country Crock with the milk, pasta, chicken, veggies & cheddar cheese in a 8-inch baking dish.
  2. combine bread crumbs, parmesan cheese & remaining Country Crock in a small bowl; sprinkle on top. bake 30 mins. or until heated through.
