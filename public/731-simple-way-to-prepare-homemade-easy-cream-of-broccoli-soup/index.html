The ingredients needed to make Easy Cream Of Broccoli Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Take 1/2 yellow onion, dice
  3. Get 2 carrots, diced
  4. Take 2 heads broccoli crowns (roughly 8 cups), chopped
  5. Get 3 cups vegetable broth (can also use chicken stock if not vegeta
  6. Make ready 1 tsp sea salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup parmesan cheese, grated (can use nutritional yeast
  9. Take 1/4 cup plain greek yoghurt (can use non-dairy yoghurt , if vegan
Instructions to make Easy Cream Of Broccoli Soup:
  1. Heat the olive oil in a large, deep stock pot over medium-high heat
  2. Add the onion and carrot and cook until tender, about 4-6 minutes.
  3. Add the broccoli, vegetable broth, salt, pepper, and parmesan cheese and cook, covered, for 15-20 minutes, or until broccoli is completely tender.
  4. Use an immersion blender (or a kitchen blender, in batches) to completely puree the broccoli soup mixture
  5. Once pureed, add the greek yoghurt and stir to incorporate
  6. Heat through before serving
