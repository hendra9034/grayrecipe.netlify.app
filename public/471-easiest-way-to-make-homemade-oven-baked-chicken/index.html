Steps to make Oven baked chicken:
  1. To marinate the chicken, add all the spices mentioned above to the chicken and mix well
  2. Add the crushed garlic, and add the oil. Mix well again
  3. Cover the mixture and put in the refrigerator
  4. Allow to sit for 45 minutes or more
  5. Preheat the oven
  6. Transfer mixture into an oven baked bowl and Add half of the sliced green pepper
  7. Put in the oven and allow to bake at 450 degrees for 25 minutes
  8. Bring it out and cover with a clingfilm paper
  9. Put back into the oven and allow to cook for another 25 minutes
  10. Garnish with the rest of the sliced green pepper
  11. Serve hot and enjoy (can also serve with potatoes, rice, couscous etc)
