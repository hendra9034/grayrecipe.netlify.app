The ingredients needed to make Stir fry Chicken Sesame Pasta:
  1. Take Spaghetti pasta
  2. Prepare Chicken Breast Fillet, cut into strips or cubed
  3. Take Oyster Saice
  4. Take Sugar
  5. Prepare Hoisin Sauce
  6. Make ready Chili sauce (or Sriracha or pepper flakes)
  7. Get Peanut butter
  8. Make ready Cornstarch
  9. Take Butter
  10. Take Shitake mushroom, sliced
  11. Take Gloves Garlic, sliced
  12. Take Onion, chopped
  13. Take Spring Onion, cut 1in. long
  14. Take Sesame oil
  15. Make ready Freshly Ground pepper
  16. Take Unsalted Peanut
Steps to make Stir fry Chicken Sesame Pasta:
  1. Combine Oyster Sauce, Hoisin sauce, Brown sugar, pepper reserve 1 Tbsp before adding chilli sauce and peanut butter. Mix.
  2. Marinate chicken with the reserve misture and cornstarch.
  3. Cook pasta according to package
  4. Heat pan add 1Tbsp Sesame oil and butter
  5. Saute onion & garlic
  6. Add chicken, saute for 3mins.
  7. Add mushroom, saute for another 3-5 mins.
  8. Pour in sauce mixture (adjust according to taste)
  9. Add in peanuts, pasta and spring onion.
  10. Turn off heat, add remaining sesame oil and mix.
