Steps to make One-pot creamy chicken mushroom pasta:
  1. Cook the pasta in hot water + 1 teaspoon of salt for 9-11 minutes then drain butðŸ§Š keep a cup of its waterðŸ§Š
  2. In a big pan, heat the oil and butter and fry the chicken until cooked and move it to the side of the pan
  3. Add the chopped onion and garlic and cook for 2 minutes then add the mushroom and cook for two more minutes then mix with the chicken and add salt, black pepper, oregano and paprika
  4. Add the cream and pasta water to the mixture then the pasta and let it simmer for ~5 minutes on medium heat
  5. Add the mozzarella cheese. Mix and turn off the heat after 2 more minutes
  6. Serve and add a sprinkle of parsley and parmesan. Enjoy!
