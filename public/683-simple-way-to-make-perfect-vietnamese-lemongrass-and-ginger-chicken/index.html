The ingredients needed to make Vietnamese lemongrass and ginger chicken:
  1. Prepare 3 large chicken breasts
  2. Get 6 cloves garlic - finely chopped
  3. Prepare 3 sticks lemon grass - finely chopped
  4. Take 2 small onions - 1 finely chopped, the other finely sliced
  5. Make ready 1 large knob ginger - finely grated Parmesan
  6. Get 3 tsp turmeric
  7. Make ready 5 tbsp Vietnamese fish sauce (3 tbsp if you can only find Thai)
  8. Get 1 tsp caster sugar
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp siracha / hot chilli sauce
  10. Take 1 1/2 tbsp oil, plus extra for frying
  11. Take 1 chilli finely chopped
  12. Get 2 tbsp fresh coriander

Instructions to make Vietnamese lemongrass and ginger chicken:
  1. Finely chop the lemongrass, garlic, ginger. Slice / chop the onion. Set aside.
  2. Slice the chicken breasts into strips and put in a bag or bowl. Add 1/3 of the lemongrass, garlic and all of the ginger. Then add the turmeric, fish sauce, sugar, siracha, oil and a little black pepper. Mix together and set aside to marinade for 30 minutes.
  3. After 30 mins, heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan over a medium heat. Add the chopped and sliced onion, remaining lemongrass and remaining garlic. Fry until the onion is soft. Be careful not to burn them.
  4. Next add the chicken, all of the marinading juices, finely chopped chilli and 150 ml water (you might need more or less water depending on how big your chicken breasts are). You want enough to create a light sauce.
  5. Simmer for 7-10 minutes until the chicken has cooked through. Sprinkle over the coriander and serve with rice (and some green veg if you fancy it)
