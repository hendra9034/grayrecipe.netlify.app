The ingredients needed to make Sweet&Sour chicken hong kong style:
  1. Take Veg tray, half onion,green pepper,carrot, 8 chunks of pinappl
  2. Prepare chicken breasts cut into bite size pieces
  3. Get egg beaten,1 tbsp shaoxing wine,salt,pepper,sugar,msg
  4. Prepare Cornflour
  5. Make ready Sweet and sour sauce mix is half a cup of tomato kechup,vinegar
  6. Make ready a cup of light brown sugar,half a cup of pinapple juice
  7. Get a cup of water, 1 tbsp cornflour
Steps to make Sweet&Sour chicken hong kong style:
  1. Mix all the wine and beaten egg and msg,salt,pepper and marinate chicken
  2. Fry in veg oil at 160c for 10 mins when you have put in cornflour
  3. Then drop it in the sweet and sour sauce and serve with rice
