The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Chicken Cheese Baked Rice:
  1. Get 1 cup rice
  2. Prepare 300 gm chicken breast (cubed)
  3. Prepare 200 gm fresh mushrooms (cubed)
  4. Take to taste Pepper, salt, paprika
  5. Prepare 1 cup cheese (mozzarella) (cubed)
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp grated cheese
  7. Make ready Butter
Instructions to make Mushroom Chicken Cheese Baked Rice:
  1. Cook rice in rice cooker (1 cup rice, 2 cups water) with salt and pepper.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare oven for 200°c and heat butter in a pan. Stir fry chicken with mushroom, add paprika, salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add rice when mushrooms start releasing water. Mix for a while and remove from heat. Add mozzarella and mix, then transfer all to a baking pan lined with baking paper.
  4. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes (until mozzarella melted) and sprinkle grated cheese on top (and butter, if needed) and bake for additional 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot. Enjoy!
