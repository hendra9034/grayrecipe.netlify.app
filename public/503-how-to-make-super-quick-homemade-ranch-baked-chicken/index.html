Instructions to make Ranch baked chicken:
  1. Cut chicken breasts in half to thin them. You want each piece about 4-5 ounces. Heat oven to 400.
  2. Mix your Parmesan, bread crumbs and ranch packet in a bowl and set aside. Cover both sides of chicken in mayo and cover in your bread crumb mixture.
  3. Place on foil lined baking sheet and bake 30-40 minutes until juices run clear
