Instructions to make Sweet, sour and spicy chicken wings:
  1. Wash and drain the chicken wings, on the wings add grated veggies, seasoning cubes, spices and salt 1. Add, the soy sauce, vinegar and honey, mix well, then add in the chopped curry leaves.mix well and allow to marinate for 1 hour
  2. In a hot pan drizzle oil and place wings, cover the pan and allow to cook for 7 minute on low heat, flip over the wing and allow to cook for 5 minutes,pour in the remaining marinade and mix, allow to cook for few minutes.
  3. Serve and garnish with curry leaves
