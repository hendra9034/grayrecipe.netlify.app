Steps to make LG SWEET AND SOUR CHICKEN

(ASIAN STYLE COOKING ):

  1. MARINATE CHICKEN OR PORK
  2. marinate chicken strip for 5 minute
  3. CHICKEN
  4. on a bowl with cornstarch dip marinated chicken strip then deep fry till lightly brown and cooked about 3 minute or longer depending on the thickness and size of the chicken strip
  5. SWEET AND SOUR SAUCE
  6. in pan with 1 tbsp of oil saute carrot and onion and red chili pepper for 2 minute
  7. add sweet and sour sauce and bring it to a boil and then add ( cornstarch diluted water ) mix well and off heat
  8. add cooked deep fried chicken strip and cucumber and toass around and coat well then serve immediately
  9. sweet and sour chicken serve with white rice
