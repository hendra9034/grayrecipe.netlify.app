The ingredients needed to make Sweet and sour chicken:
  1. Prepare 1 slice Onion
  2. Get 1 tbsp Olive oil
  3. Get 2 clove Garlic ( minced)
  4. Get 6 Chicken breasts
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup Honey
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Salt
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup Grapefruit juice
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Cinnamon
  9. Make ready 2 tsp Curry powder
  10. Prepare 1 Zucchini (sliced)
  11. Get 1 Zucchini (sliced)
  12. Prepare 1 Red bell pepper, sliced
  13. Take 1 can Crushed pineapple
  14. Make ready 2 tbsp Toasted sesame seeds

Instructions to make Sweet and sour chicken:
  1. Saute onion in olive oil. Add garlic and chicken; continue to saute until chicken breasts are browned. Mix honey, salt, grapefruit.juice and spices. Pour juice mixture over chicken & simmer 30 minutes
  2. Add sliced vegetables and pineapple and simmer 10 minutes or until vegetables are crisp tender. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve
