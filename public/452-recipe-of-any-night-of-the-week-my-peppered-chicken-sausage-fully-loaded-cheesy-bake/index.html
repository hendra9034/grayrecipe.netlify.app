Steps to make My peppered Chicken, Sausage fully loaded cheesy Bake:
  1. Half grill the sausage.
  2. Slice the sausage into small pieces
  3. Melt butter in frying pan then add the onions and Garlic fry for 1 minute then add the chicken just to colour also paprika and pepper flakes and Cajun seadoning
  4. Paprika
  5. Pepper flakes
  6. Cajun spice
  7. Add the sausage to the chicken
  8. Turn heat off and stir in the sausage.
  9. In a roasting greased oven dish, about 3 inches Depth, add the chicken and veg including potatoe Add all the veg and bell Peppers pour in the chicken stock.
  10. Stir to mix in and add the sweet peppers
  12. Courgettes
  13. Potatoe mix in
  14. Add the chedder Cheese
  15. Then add the monteray jack cheese
  16. Lastly add 1 tsp Black cracked pepper over the cheese and Worcestershire sauce. Sprinkle all over
  17. Worsestershire sauce
  18. Cover with foil and add it to a preheated oven 170C, Gas 6 for 45 minutes. Try not to stick on the cheese.
  19. Uncover and put it back in for 10 minutes.
  20. Serve Hot
