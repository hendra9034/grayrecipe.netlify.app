The ingredients needed to make 3hr Bake Forgotten Chicken & Rice:
  1. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken soup
  2. Get 1 can cream of celery soup
  3. Prepare 1 envelope onion soup mix
  4. Make ready 1 can water
  5. Get 1 packages chicken breast (4 pieces)
  6. Get 1 box rice
Steps to make 3hr Bake Forgotten Chicken & Rice:
  1. preheat oven to 275
  2. mix cans of soup, 1 can of water, and rice together in a deep baking pan
  3. place chicken on top of mixture
  4. sprinkle onion soup mix on top
  5. cover with foil and cook for 3 hours
