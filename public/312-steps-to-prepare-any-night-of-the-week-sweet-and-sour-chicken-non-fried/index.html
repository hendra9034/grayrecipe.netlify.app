The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Chicken (Non-Fried):
  1. Prepare 1 Chicken thigh (divided into 12 pieces)
  2. Make ready 1 Salt (for pre-seasoning)
  3. Prepare 1 Pepper (for pre-seasoning)
  4. Prepare 1 Ginger (grated / for pre-seasoning)
  5. Take 2 tbsp Katakuriko
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp Vegetable oil
  7. Prepare 1/2 Carrot ( roughly sliced)
  8. Get 1 Onion (cut into wedges)
  9. Take 3 Green Pepper (roughly sliced)
  10. Get Combined seasoning ingredients:
  11. Get 200 ml Water
  12. Prepare 4 tbsp Vinegar
  13. Take 3 tbsp Tomato ketchup
  14. Prepare 3 tbsp White sugar
  15. Take 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  16. Get 1/2 tsp Mustard paste
  17. Take 1 tbsp Katakuriko
Instructions to make Sweet and Sour Chicken (Non-Fried):
  1. Put the chicken thigh in a plastic bag and season it. Add the katakuriko and coat the chicken by shaking the bag.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan and spread meat. Put a lid on and fry over medium heat for 3 minutes. Flip it over and fry for another 3 minutes. Serve it on a plate.
  3. Microwave carrots for 2 minutes at 600 W.
  4. In the same pan with remaining oil, fry carrots, onion and pepper.
  5. When they have wilted, add the mixed flavoring ingredients and simmer.
  6. Once the sauce has thickened, mix in the meat.
