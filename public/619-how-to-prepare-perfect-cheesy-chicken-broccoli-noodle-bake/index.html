The ingredients needed to make Cheesy chicken broccoli noodle bake:
  1. Make ready 3 each chopped cooked chicken breasts
  2. Get 2 cup broccoli florets
  3. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken soup
  4. Make ready 1 can cheddar cheese soup
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 2 cup cooked wide egg noodles
  7. Make ready 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (or any other cheese)
  8. Get 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  9. Get 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  10. Get 1 Sleeve Crushed Ritz Crackers
  11. Get 4 tbsp melted butter

Steps to make Cheesy chicken broccoli noodle bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 째
  2. Boil chicken in large stock pot
  3. Mix soups, shredded cheese, salt, pepper and garlic together in medium bowl
  4. Boil noodles according to pkg
  5. Boil broccoli according to pkg
  6. Cut chicken into cubes
  7. Mix all ingredients in large baking dish. (9x13")
  8. Top with crushed crackers
  9. Drizzle melted butter over top of crackers
  10. Bake for 30 minutes in 350째 oven
