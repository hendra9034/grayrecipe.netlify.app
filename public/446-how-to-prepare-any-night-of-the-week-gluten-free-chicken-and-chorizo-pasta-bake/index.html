The ingredients needed to make Gluten free chicken and chorizo pasta bake:
  1. Make ready 200 g gluten free pasta
  2. Make ready 1 tablespoon olive oil
  3. Make ready 2 chicken breasts, cubed
  4. Take 1 onion, diced
  5. Make ready 1 red pepper, diced
  6. Make ready 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  7. Take 100 g chorizo sliced
  8. Take 300 g passata
  9. Get 125 ml cream
  10. Take 2 teaspoons mixed herbs
  11. Prepare salt and pepper for seasoning
  12. Get Handful grated cheese grated

Steps to make Gluten free chicken and chorizo pasta bake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Bring a large pan of salted water to the boil and cook the pasta for 8 minutes
  3. In a large pan place the olive oil and garlic on a medium heat
  4. Add the chorizo and chicken and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Add in the onion and pepper and saute for 5 minutes
  6. In a small saucepan place the passata, cream, mixed herbs and season with salt and pepper
  7. Bring to the boil and simmer for 5 minutes
  8. Pour the sauce and pasta into the chorizo and chicken mix
  9. Place into a casserole dish and top with the grated cheese
  10. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes
