The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken and Broccoli Bake:
  1. Get Main Ingredients
  2. Take 4 Skinned Boneless Chicken Breast
  3. Make ready 225 grams Broccoli Florets
  4. Make ready Sauce
  5. Prepare 50 grams Salad Cream
  6. Get 1 tsp curry powder
  7. Make ready 300 ml Chicken and Leek soup or Chicken soup
  8. Take 10 ml Skimmed Milk
Instructions to make Creamy Chicken and Broccoli Bake:
  1. Cook Broccoli in boiling water until tender. If using frozen broccoli there is no need to boil.
  2. Place chicken breast and broccoli in a shallow oven period dish.
  3. Pour the soup over the chicken and brocolli.
  4. In a small bowl combine together Salad Cream, Curry and Milk. And pour over the chicken and brocoli coating them well
  5. Bake at 190°C/ 375°F for about 55 -60 min.
