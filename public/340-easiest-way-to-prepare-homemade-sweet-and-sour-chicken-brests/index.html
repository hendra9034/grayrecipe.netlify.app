Instructions to make sweet and sour chicken brests:
  1. preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. place (thawed) chicken breasts in large cake pan
  3. mix ketchup, apple juice, brown sugar and lemon juice in small pot
  4. heat ingredients till thickened
  5. pour sauce mixture over chicken breasts and cook for 75 mins till chicken is fully cooked
  6. let cool down and serve
