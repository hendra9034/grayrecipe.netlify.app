  4. Prepare 1 cup chicken cooked and sliced

  6. Take 1/2 medium red pepper diced small
  7. Get 1 tsp garlic salt
  8. Take 1 tbsp hot peppersauce

  3. saute peppers onion and chicken on Med heat for 5 to 7 mins.
  4. add pineapple pepper sauce garlic salt and raisins to chicken mixture stirring til mixed
  5. serve hot over rice
