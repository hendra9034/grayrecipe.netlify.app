The ingredients needed to make Baked cordon Bleu with creamy chicken corn sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 chicken breast
  2. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  3. Make ready to taste Garlic salt
  4. Take 4 slices Mozzarella cheese(or any cheese)
  5. Make ready Bread crumbs/flour/corn meal
  6. Prepare Melted margarine
  7. Take Sauce:
  8. Take 2 tbsp cream of chicken soup powder
  9. Take 4 tbsp canned in corn
  10. Take 1/2 cup heavy cream
  11. Take 1/4-1/2 cup milk

Instructions to make Baked cordon Bleu with creamy chicken corn sauce:
  1. Cut up chicken breast into fillets, salt and pepper them on both sides.
  2. Roll out one piece of chicken at a time in the flour/breading mixture until thin enough making sure to get both sides covered in crumbs.
  3. Add a slice of cheese onto every piece of chicken and roll closed, insert toothpicks to keep closed.
  4. Dip the chicken rolls into the melted margarine. Place on baking sheet. Bake in 350° oven for about 45 minutes or until done.
  5. Sauce: mix all ingredients in a small saucepan and bring to a boil, if sauce is too thick add more milk.
  6. Pour sauce over chicken and serve with mashed potatoes. ENJOY!
