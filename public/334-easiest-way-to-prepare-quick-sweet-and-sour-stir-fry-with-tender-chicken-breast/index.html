The ingredients needed to make Sweet and Sour Stir-fry with Tender Chicken Breast:
  1. Get 2 Chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 packet Shimeji mushrooms
  3. Take 1 ●Salt and pepper
  4. Get 1 tbsp ●Sake
  5. Take 2 tbsp Katakuriko
  6. Take 1 tbsp Oil
  7. Make ready 2 clove ◎Garlic (grated)
  8. Get 3 tbsp ◎ Ketchup
  9. Prepare 4 tbsp ◎ Ponzu
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp ◎ Sugar
Steps to make Sweet and Sour Stir-fry with Tender Chicken Breast:
  1. Slice the chicken breasts and cut into big bite- sizes. Put in a bowl and add the ● ingredients. Rub well. Add the katakuriko and rub until there is no excess flour left.
  2. Combine the ◎ ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan and arrange the prepared chicken pieces in one layer. Fry over medium heat.
  4. Add the shimeji mushrooms and the ◎ sauce. When the sauce is thickened, it is done.
