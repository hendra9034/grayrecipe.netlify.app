The ingredients needed to make Easy Italian Baked Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 lb thin sliced chicken breast
  2. Get to taste Pepper
  3. Take 1 yellow onion
  4. Make ready to taste garlic powder
  5. Prepare to taste italian seasoning
  6. Take 1 clove garlic
  7. Prepare to taste salt
  8. Make ready 7 vine cherry tomatoes
  9. Make ready 3 tablespoons olive oil
Instructions to make Easy Italian Baked Chicken:
  1. Pre heat oven to 370 degrees F
  2. Align baking sheet with aluminum foil
  3. Slice yellow onion into thin rings
  4. Place half of the rings directly on the baking sheet
  5. Drizzle baking sheet with olive oil
  6. Place chicken on baking sheet
  7. Season chicken to taste with all seasonings and herbs listed
  8. Place the rest of the yellow onion rings on top of chicken
  9. Chop the garlic clove and sprinkle on chicken evenly
  10. Quarter cherry tomatoes on a cutting board
  11. Place quartered tomatoes on the baking sheet and season with italian seasoning
  12. Place baking sheet in oven for 40 minutes or until onion rings begin to brown
