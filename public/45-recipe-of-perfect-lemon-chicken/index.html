The ingredients needed to make Lemon Chicken:
  1. Take 300 g boneless and skinless chicken thigh
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  3. Get I tablespoon of chopped ginger
  4. Take I tablespoon of chopped garlic
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  6. Get 1 teaspoon white pepper
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon light soya sauce
  8. Prepare I tablespoon of dark soya sauce
  9. Make ready I tablespoon of black or white sesame seeds
  10. Prepare I tablespoon of chopped chilli
  11. Get I tablespoon of chopped spring onion
  12. Make ready Some fresh coriander
Instructions to make Lemon Chicken:
  1. Heat up the non-stick wok, put the boneless chicken in the non-stick wok.
  2. When the chicken is lightly cooked on the surface, use a scissor and cut the chicken into easy bite pieces, add a teaspoon of sesame oil, a teaspoon of salt and a teaspoon of pepper on the chicken
  3. Add ginger, garlic and red chilli and gently sauté for 1 minute until fragrant. Stir the chicken and add 1 tablespoon of light soya sauce and 1 tablespoon of dark sauce sauce and cook on 5 minutes on the medium heat.
  4. When the chicken is cooked for another 10 minutes, cut the lemon into half, remove the seeds and squeeze the lemon juice on the chicken. Pour honey and sesame seeds and toss it until it is evenly coated. - - Garnish with coriander, red chilli and spring onions and ready to serve.
