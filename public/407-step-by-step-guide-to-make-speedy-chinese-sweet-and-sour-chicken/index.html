The ingredients needed to make Chinese Sweet- and- Sour Chicken:
  1. Prepare 20 oz pineapple chunks in juice
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
  3. Get 1/4 cup rice vinegar or white wine vinegar
  4. Get 3 tbsp soy sauce
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp cornstarch
  6. Get 1 tbsp preminced oil-packed garlic
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp jarred grated fresh ginger
  8. Make ready 2 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  9. Get 1 cup prechopped tricolor bell pepper
  10. Get 1 cup prechopped onion
  11. Take 1/2 cup baby carrots
Steps to make Chinese Sweet- and- Sour Chicken:
  1. Coat the inside of a 5- to 6-quart slow cooker pot with cooking spray.
  2. Drain the pineapple and set it aside, reserving 1 cup of juice. In the pot, combine the sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, cornstarch, garlic, ginger, and the reserved juice. Whisk until smooth. Add the chicken, pepper, onion, and carrots. Stir to coat with sauce. Cover and cook on the low-heat setting for 5 to 6 hours. Stir in the reserved pineapple. Cook for 5 minutes, or until the pineapple is heated.
  3. Note: Chinese Sweet-and-Sour Chicken can also be cooked on the high-heat setting for 2 1/2 to 3 hours.
