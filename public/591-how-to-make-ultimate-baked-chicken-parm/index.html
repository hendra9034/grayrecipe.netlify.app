The ingredients needed to make Baked Chicken Parm:
  1. Take 4 Chicken breast
  2. Make ready 1 Egg
  3. Take 1/4 cup Water
  4. Get 1 cup Bread crumbs
  5. Take 4 tbsp Parm cheese
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Oregano
  7. Take 1/2 tsp Basil
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  9. Take 1/2 tsp Thyme
  10. Get 2 cup Sauce
  11. Prepare 1 cup Mozzarella cheese
  12. Get 1 cup Flour
  13. Prepare 1 cup Spaghetti sauce
Steps to make Baked Chicken Parm:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Spray pan with cooking spray
  3. Whisk water and egg in flat bowl
  4. Combine Parm cheese, seasoning and bread crumbs in flat bowl
  5. Dredge flatten chicken in flower than egg than bread crumb mixture
  6. Cook chicken for 40 min
  7. Add sauce than cheese to chicken and cook for 15 min
