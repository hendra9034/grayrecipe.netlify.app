The ingredients needed to make Korean Style Sticky Chilli Chicken:
  1. Make ready For the chicken:
  2. Take 2 large chicken breasts, chopped into bite sized pieces,
  3. Take 4 heaped tbsp cornflour,
  4. Get 1 tsp white pepper,
  5. Take 1 tsp table salt,
  6. Get For the sauce:
  7. Prepare 2 heaped tbsp gochujang paste,
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp runny honey,
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp seasoned rice vinegar,
  10. Take 2 tbsp light soy sauce,
  11. Take 1/2 tbsp finely chopped fresh root ginger,
  12. Make ready 2 tsp garlic purée paste,
  13. Get 3 tsp sesame seeds,
  14. Make ready 1 tsp sesame oil
  15. Take To garnish:
  16. Get 4 spring onions, chopped finely, whites and greens seperated,
  17. Take Other:
  18. Take Cooking oil for shallow frying

Instructions to make Korean Style Sticky Chilli Chicken:
  1. Season the cornflour with salt and pepper then toss the chicken pieces into it to coat.
  2. Heat up the oil in a large pan and cover the base by around 1/2 inch, enough to shallow fry. Once hot, (the chicken should immediately sizzle when added), add the chicken pieces in batches and cook until golden, crispy and cooked through.
  3. Mix together all of the sauce ingredients until smooth and add in the whites of the onions too, mix through and set aside. Once the chicken is cooked, use a slotted spoon or tongs to carefully transfer it to some kitchen towel to remove the excess oil.
  4. Heat up a wok over a medium heat and add in the sauce. Bring to a simmer and let it thicken and reduce down by 1/3rd. Toss the cooked chicken pieces in the sauce until fully coated. Add in half the greens of the spring onions and scatter over a few extra sesame seeds. Toss through once more to incorporate.
  5. Garnish with the remaining greens of the spring onions. Recommend to serve up over steamed white rice. Eat and enjoy! :)
