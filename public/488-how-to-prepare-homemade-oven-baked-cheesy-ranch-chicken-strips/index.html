The ingredients needed to make Oven Baked Cheesy-Ranch Chicken Strips:
  1. Make ready 6 Boneless chicken Breast
  2. Make ready 1 Ranch dip packet (powder form)
  3. Prepare 1 sleeve of RITZ crackers
  4. Prepare 2/3 cup bread crums
  5. Make ready 1 tsp salt & pepper
  6. Get 1 tsp dry minced onions
  7. Get 2/3 cup parmesan cheese
  8. Get 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  9. Get 1 tsp oregano
  10. Get 2 cup milk
Instructions to make Oven Baked Cheesy-Ranch Chicken Strips:
  1. preheat oven 375Â°F
  2. Put Milk in a large bowl
  3. In a different bowl mix all dry ingredients EXCEPT the cheddar cheese
  4. Slice chicken breasts into 3rds
  5. dip chicken slices into milk then into the bread crum mix
  6. oil the baking pan then place each strip side by side
  7. put in the oven for 30mins
  8. check to be sure its done then put the cheddar on top and put back in oven for 2mins
  9. then serve
