The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Stuffed Baked Chicken:
  1. Get 3 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  2. Prepare to taste Salt
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Black pepper
  4. Make ready 1 cup mozzarella
  5. Make ready 2 cups flour
  6. Prepare 6 eggs beaten
  7. Take 2 cups bread crumbs
  8. Take 1 cup oil for frying
  9. Get 3 cups tomato sauce
  10. Get 3 piece cheese slice
  11. Prepare 2 tsp basil
  12. Make ready 2 tsp origano
  13. Prepare 2 tsp parsley
Steps to make Cheesy Stuffed Baked Chicken:
  1. Wash the chicken and marnated to salt and pepper for 10-15 min.
  2. Cut a packet into each chicken breast. - Stuff the pockets evenly with the mozzarella cheese.
  3. Press the edges of the chicken together to seal the packet.
  4. Separate the flour, eggs, and bread crumbs into 3 separate bowls.
  5. Being careful to keep the chicken from opening, dip the stuffed chicken in the flour, shaking off the excess.
  6. Dip the floured chicken into the egg, then the bread crumbs, coating it evenly.
  7. Heat the oil in a large pan over medium heat. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  8. Fry the chicken until golden brown on both sides.
  9. Place ⅔ of the tomato sauce evenly on the bottom of a baking dish. Place the chicken on top.
  10. Top with the rest of the tomato sauce, then sprinkle the basil,origano & parsley & the cheese slice on top.
  11. Bake for 20 minutes. - Serve!
