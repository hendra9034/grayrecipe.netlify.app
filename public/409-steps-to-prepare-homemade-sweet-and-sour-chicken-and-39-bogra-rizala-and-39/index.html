The ingredients needed to make Sweet and sour chicken 'Bogra Rizala':
  1. Prepare 1 Crispy fried onion using 3 small onions 90gm@
  2. Make ready 2 small Onions, chopped
  3. Make ready 2 Inch fresh ginger, peeled and chopped
  4. Prepare 4 clove Garlic, peeled and chopped
  5. Make ready 4 tbsp Oil saved from frying onions
  6. Get 1 tbsp Ghee our extra oil
  7. Make ready 1 3/4 kg Chicken, skinned and in small pieces (our drums)
  8. Make ready 6 tbsp Natural yoghurt
  9. Prepare 6 10 whole birds eye chillis with small slits cut in them
  10. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  11. Prepare 1 3/4 tsp Sugar
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp Lemon or lime juice
Instructions to make Sweet and sour chicken 'Bogra Rizala':
  1. Crumble fried onions and set aside
  2. Put the chopped onions, ginger, garlic and 3-4 tablespoons water into a blender and blend until you have a smooth paste
  3. Put the oil and ghee in a large non-stick, lidded fry pan and set over a medium high heat.when hot, poor in the paste from the blender and stir-fry for about 5 mins until the paste is lightly browned.
  4. Add the chicken pieces, stir for a further minute.
  5. Over the next 5 minutes add the yoghurt 1 tbsp at a time and keep browning the chicken.
  6. Add 175 mls water, the crumbled onions and salt. Sure to mix and bring to a simmer.
  7. Cover, reduce the heat to low and simmer gently for 20 mins, turning the chicken every now and then
  8. Add the chillies, sugar and juice, stir to mix. Cover and continue to chok on very low heat for another 10 minutes.
