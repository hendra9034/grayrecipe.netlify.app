The ingredients needed to make Heather's Baked Chicken 2:
  1. Prepare 3 boneless skinless chicken breast
  2. Get 2 can cream of chicken soup (10.75oz cans)
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup butter
Steps to make Heather's Baked Chicken 2:
  1. preheat oven to 375°
  2. heat butter and soup together on the stove or in a microwave safe dish. place chicken breast in a baking dish and pour soup mixture over the chicken. cover with aluminum foil and bake 30 minutes cover. remove foil and bake and additional 15 minutes
