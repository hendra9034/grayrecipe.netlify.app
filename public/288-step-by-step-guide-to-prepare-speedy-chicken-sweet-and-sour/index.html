The ingredients needed to make Chicken Sweet And Sour:
  1. Make ready chicken breasts, cubed
  2. Make ready cornflour
  3. Get egg
  4. Take salt
  5. Make ready tomato sauce
  6. Make ready chilli sauce
  7. Prepare soya sauce
  8. Get ginger-garlic paste
  9. Prepare oil to deep fry
  10. Prepare garlic, grated
  11. Take assorted bell peppers, cubed
  12. Get spring onion (white part)
  13. Make ready onion, cubed
  14. Get salt
  15. Get pepper powder
  16. Get vinegar
  17. Make ready cornflour mixed in 1/2 cup water
  18. Prepare chopped spring onion (green part)
Steps to make Chicken Sweet And Sour:
  1. Marinate the chicken with salt to taste, egg, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste and all the sauces for 10-15 minutes. Heat oil to deep fry.
  2. Heat oil to deep fry. Fry the marinated chicken cubes in batches till golden. Drain on a kitchen towel and keep aside.
  3. Heat 1 tbsp. oil in a pan and temper with garlic. Saute for a few seconds. Add cubed veggies, onion, spring onion, salt & pepper. Stir fry for 2-3 min.
  4. Add the cornflour syrup, vinegar.
  5. And the fried chicken. Continue to simmer till it becomes slightly thick.
  6. Switch off the flame and garnish with the chopped spring onion.
  7. Serve on a bed of noodles or with egg fried rice.
