The ingredients needed to make Chicken Alfredo Baked Ziti "cheesy":
  1. Make ready 2 box Ziti noodles
  2. Make ready 1 packages Thin cut chicken breast
  3. Prepare 2 cup Need to buy 2 large cups of ricotta cheese
  4. Take 2 Eggs
  5. Get 1 packages Shredded mozzarella cheese
  6. Make ready 2 packages Italian style shredded cheese
  7. Make ready 1 packages Parmesan shredded cheese
  8. Prepare Italian seasoning
  9. Get Garlic and onion powder
  10. Take Salt and pepper
  11. Prepare Parsley flakes
  12. Make ready 2 can Ragu Alfredo sauce
Steps to make Chicken Alfredo Baked Ziti "cheesy":
  1. Boil water once boiled add ziti noodles and a pinch of salt
  2. While noodles boil start to rinse the chicken cut fat of and cut in small pieces
  3. Put a little butter in pan cook chicken season with Italian seasoning salt and pepper
  4. By the time your chicken is cooked noodles should be done drain noodles put in a large tin pan
  5. Put Both cups of cheese in a large bowl with 2 eggs stir and season add a handful of shredded mozzarella and stir
  6. Add the ricotta cheese from bowl to tin pan and stir
  7. Add 2 jars of the Alfredo sauce and stir then add all packages of cheese save a hand full
  8. Drizzle the top with sauce cover with cheese and sprinkle parsley flakes
  9. Oven on 400 for 40 minutes
