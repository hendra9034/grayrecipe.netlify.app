The ingredients needed to make Ginger Pork Takikomigohan:
  1. Take Pork *thinly sliced and cut into small pieces
  2. Take tablespoonfuls finely sliced Ginger *sliced into fine strips
  3. Make ready Soy Sauce
  4. Make ready Mirin
  5. Take Sesame Oil
  6. Get White Pepper
  7. Take (*180ml cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  8. Take Chicken Stock *OR 400ml Water & 1 teaspoon Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  9. Get Salt *alter the amount depending on the saltiness of the Stock
  10. Take Spring Onions *finely chopped
  11. Prepare Shichimi (Japanese Chilli Spice Mix) *optional
  12. Prepare Toasted Nori *optional
Steps to make Ginger Pork Takikomigohan:
  1. Thinly slice Pork and cut into small pieces, add Ginger, Soy Sauce, Mirin, Sesame Oil and White Pepper, and massage. Set aside for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Wash Rice a few times, drain well, and place it in the rice cooker. Add Chicken Stock, sprinkle with Salt, and gently stir.
  3. Place Pork pieces with all liquid on top of the Rice, but DO NOT stir. Just spread evenly. Press ‘COOK’ button to start cooking. *Note: You may wish to add Vegetables such as Carrot and Asian Mushrooms.
  4. When rice is cooked, let it steamed for 10 minutes. Add Spring Onion and mix gently. Serve with refreshing Salad or steamed Vegetables.
  5. *Note: You can add Shichimi (Japanese Chilli Spice Mix) and Toasted Nori as topping.
