Instructions to make Thyme and Parmesan crusted chicken and new potatoes:
  1. Get ingredients ready. Wash chicken and potatoes and dry well
  2. Put flour on a plate. Beat 2 eggs on a plate. Mix equal amounts of Parmesan and breadcrumbs
  3. Dip chicken in flour, then egg and then breadcrumbs. Place chicken on a lightly greased baking tray
  4. Put potatoes in a bowl. Drizzle with oil and add any leftover breadcrumb mix. Mix with a spoon
  5. Add potatoes to tray. Sprinkle with thyme. Bake at 200 for 40 mins.
  6. Turn half way and up the heat for 10 mins to brown and crisp up. Yummy :)
