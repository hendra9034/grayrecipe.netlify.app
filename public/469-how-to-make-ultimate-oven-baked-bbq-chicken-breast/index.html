The ingredients needed to make oven baked bbq chicken breast:
  1. Prepare 4 boneless skinless chicken breast
  2. Take 1 onion sliced
  3. Get 3 tbsp garlic powder
  4. Get 1 salt
  5. Get 1 pepper
  6. Make ready 1 bottle bbq sauce of choice
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp butter

Instructions to make oven baked bbq chicken breast:
  1. preheat oven 400
  2. in a skillet melt butter add onions cook until soft
  3. arrange Chicken in a baking dish season with garlic powder salt and pepper
  4. cover with onions and bbq sauce
  5. cover with a lid or aluminum foil. place in oven bake for 1 hour
  6. the sauce becomes thin u can add a little more for thickness
