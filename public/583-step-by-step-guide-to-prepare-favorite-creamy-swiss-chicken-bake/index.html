The ingredients needed to make Creamy Swiss Chicken Bake:
  1. Prepare 6 each Chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Sour cream
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  4. Get 6 slice Swiss cheese
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Garlic powder
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Pepper
  8. Prepare 1 cup Finely shredded Parmesan cheese
Steps to make Creamy Swiss Chicken Bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. Pat chicken dry and place in greased 9 x 13 pan
  3. Add sliced cheese on top of chicken breasts
  4. In a bowl mix mayonnaise, sour cream, garlic powder, 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper
  5. Spread mixture over chicken breasts
  6. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese
  7. Cook for 1 hour and serve.
