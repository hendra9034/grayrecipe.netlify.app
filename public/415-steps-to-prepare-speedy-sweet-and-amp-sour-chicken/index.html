The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Get 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons peanut oil
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup pineapple chunks in juice
  5. Take 1/2 cup chopped pineapple
  6. Get 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup rice vinegar
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup corn starch
  10. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon pepper
  12. Take 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  13. Get 1/4 cup water
Steps to make Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Pound 1 pound of boneless skinless chicken breasts to 1/2 inch thick.
  2. Cut into 1 inch cubes.
  3. Sprinkle the chicken with 1 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon pepper.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of peanut oil on a skillet over medium-high heat and spread evenly.
  5. In a large bowl, mix together
  6. When the oil heats to 350 degrees Fahrenheit, add the chicken pieces to the skillet.
  7. On a skillet over medium heat, mix 1/4 cup brown sugar and corn starch together. Add 1/3 cup rice vinegar, 1/2 cup pineapple juice, 1 tablespoon soy sauce, and 1/4 cup water. Heat to a boil for 1 minute, stirring occasionally. When the sauce thickens, transfer the liquid to a bowl.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally, then add 1/2 cup each of chopped pineapples, chopped green bell peppers, chopped white onions, and chopped carrots.
  9. Cook for an additional 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  10. Add the sweet & sour sauce to the skillet and mix.
  11. Enjoy!
