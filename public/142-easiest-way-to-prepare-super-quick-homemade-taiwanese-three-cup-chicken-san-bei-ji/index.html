The ingredients needed to make Taiwanese Three Cup Chicken (San Bei Ji):
  1. Get Chicken legs
  2. Take Ginger
  3. Prepare Garlic
  4. Make ready Some Red chilli
  5. Make ready Spring onion
  6. Get Small bunch Basil
  7. Make ready Seasonings
  8. Take Sesame oil
  9. Get Soy sauce
  10. Prepare Rice wine
  11. Prepare caster sugar
Steps to make Taiwanese Three Cup Chicken (San Bei Ji):
  1. Chop the chicken legs into proper chunks and cook them in a boiled water pan for 5 minutes. Drain the water and leave it aside. Get another pan, add in sesame oil to fry the ginger slices.
  2. Add in garlic, spring onion and chilli.
  3. Add in and fry the chicken chunks.
  4. Add in the seasonings and simmer the chicken until the sauce getting thicker.
  5. Sprinkle in some basil and mix well before serving. Enjoy. üòã
