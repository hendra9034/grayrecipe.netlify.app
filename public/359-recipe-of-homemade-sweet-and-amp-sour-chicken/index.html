The ingredients needed to make Sweet &Sour Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 large Chicken breasts
  2. Take 1 can Pineapple (you could always use fresh)
  3. Prepare 1 large Red bell pepper
  4. Get 1 large Yellow bell pepper
  5. Prepare 1 clove Chopped garlic
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp Light brown sugar
  7. Get 4 tsp Corn starch mixed with 4 teaspoons cold water
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp Rice wine vinegar
  9. Take 1/4 cup Chicken stock
  10. Take 2 dash Dried red pepper
  11. Take 1 Salt & pepper
  12. Prepare 3 tbsp Oil
Steps to make Sweet &Sour Chicken:
  1. Take a large skillet, add oil heat it up. Add chicken (cut into strips or chunks) with chopped garlic and lightly brown.
  2. Removed chicken and add chopped red & yellow bell peppers cook for 1 minute until soft.
  3. Then bring to simmer the brown sugar, pineapple juice, pineapple chunks & rice wine vinegar (can sub white wine vinegar) & chicken stock.
  4. Once reduced add the corn starch mixture slowly but keep stirring. Then add the chicken strips and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice👌(Hint: you could top with bottled sweet and sour for an extra kick)
