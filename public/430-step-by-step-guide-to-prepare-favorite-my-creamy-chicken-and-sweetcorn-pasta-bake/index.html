The ingredients needed to make My Creamy Chicken and Sweetcorn Pasta Bake:
  1. Get 1 lb chicken breasts diced(leftovers can be used)
  2. Take 500 g pasta
  3. Prepare 1 veg stock cube in 300ml hot water
  4. Make ready 1 chilli chopped
  5. Prepare 1 red onion chopped
  6. Take 1 cup frozen corn
  7. Make ready 1 cup frozen peas
  8. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
  9. Get 3 tbsp flour
  10. Get 1 cup pasta water (if needed)
  11. Make ready 1 cup milk
  12. Prepare 2 cup cheddar cheese
  13. Take 2 tsp garlic powder
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp dried parsley
  15. Get 1 tsp chilli flake
  16. Get 1 tsp mixed herbs
  17. Prepare Oil for frying chicken
  18. Get 1/2 cup grated parmigiana cheese
  19. Make ready Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions to make My Creamy Chicken and Sweetcorn Pasta Bake:
  1. Cook chicken until brown then set aside
  2. Put stock cube and frozen veg in water bring to a boil,in a separate pot cook pasta al-dente, keep 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.
  3. In a large pot melt butter, add onion and chilli sauté for 1 min add flour and cook for 1 min more then add stock with veg slowly to create the sauce
  4. When all stock with veg is in add chicken seasonings milk cheese and enough pasta water if needed for sauce
  5. Then In a large oven proof dish combine pasta and chicken in creamy sauce,sprinkle with parmigiana cheese
  6. Bake covered at 180 for 40 mins - Then uncovered for a further 10 mins until golden…..enjoy
