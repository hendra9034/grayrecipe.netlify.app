The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Chicken Breast:
  1. Make ready About 3 fillets Chicken Breast
  2. Get 1 tspn cake flour
  3. Get 1/4 cup fresh milk
  4. Get 1 tsp mild Chutney
  5. Prepare 1 tbspn all gold tomato sauce
  6. Take 1 tbspn white vinegar
  7. Get 1 1/2 tspn chicken grill spice
  8. Make ready 1/2 tspn peri peri
  9. Make ready 1/4 red onion
  10. Take 2 tbspn olive oil
Instructions to make Sweet & Sour Chicken Breast:
  1. Chop ur chicken breast into nice cubes. - Season with chicken spice and vinegar - Heat ur pot/pan and add oil. Fry ur chicken breast till brown, add oion and continue frying for about 2 minutes..
  2. Mix fresh milk with flour and add to your pot.. cook for 1-2 minutes then add peri peri powder, Chutney, tomato sauce and simmer for a minute. Enjoyüòò
