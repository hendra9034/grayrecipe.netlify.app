The ingredients needed to make Delicious and Healthy Sweet & Sour Pork With Chicken Tenders:
  1. Make ready 5 strips Chicken tenders
  2. Take 2 Green peppers
  3. Get 1 Onion
  4. Take 1/2 Carrot
  5. Get 2 pieces Shiitake mushroom
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Katakuriko
  7. Take 2 tbsp ★Ketchup
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp of each ★ Vinegar, soy sauce
  9. Take 1 tbsp of each ★Sugar, sesame oil
Instructions to make Delicious and Healthy Sweet & Sour Pork With Chicken Tenders:
  1. Cut the chicken tenders into bite sized pieces and soak in unlisted (1 tablespoon sake, 1 teaspoon soy sauce). Let soak until it's basically time to use the chicken.
  2. Cut the carrots into long, thin strips, cut the onions into 1-2 cm slices, cut the green peppers into chunks, and cut the shiitake mushrooms into bite sized pieces. Mix the ★ ingredients together.
  3. Coat a frying pan with oil, and sauté everything. Lightly sauté the green peppers last out of everything else. After sautéing, take it all out onto a plate temporarily.
  4. Stir the katakuriko into Step 1, coat the frying pan with oil, and pan-fry.
  5. If it looks like it is going to burn, add in a bit of water, and steam it. ♪ After it has cooked through, add in the plate of vegetables, coat well in the ★ ingredients, and it is done.
