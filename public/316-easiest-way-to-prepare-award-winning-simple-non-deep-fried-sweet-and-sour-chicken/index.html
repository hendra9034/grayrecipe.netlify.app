The ingredients needed to make Simple Non-Deep Fried Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Prepare 300 grams Chicken breast
  2. Make ready For marinating:
  3. Prepare 1 dash ● Salt and pepper
  4. Prepare 1 tsp ● Soy sauce
  5. Take 1 to 2 tablespoons Katakuriko
  6. Prepare 1/2 Red bell pepper
  7. Prepare 1/2 Yellow bell pepper
  8. Take 1 Onion
  9. Get The sauce ingredients:
  10. Take 1 tbsp ○ Soy sauce
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp ○ Sake
  12. Get 1 tbsp ○ Sugar
  13. Take 3 tbsp ○ Vinegar
  14. Get 2 tbsp ○ Ketchup
  15. Get 1 tsp ○ Chicken soup stock granules
  16. Make ready 1 tsp ○ Katakuriko
  17. Make ready 2 tbsp Oil
Steps to make Simple Non-Deep Fried Sweet and Sour Chicken:
  1. Mix the sauce ingredients together and set aside.
  2. Cut the vegetables and breast meat into bite-sized chunks and soak the meat in the marinating ingredients.
  3. Once one side has cooked, turn the pieces over and add about 40 ml of water, then place the lid on the frying pan and steam the chicken.
  4. Coat the chicken in the katakuriko and fry it on one side until it turns a light brown colour (don't touch the chicken too much).
  5. Once one side has cooked, turn the pieces over and add about 40 ml of water, then place the lid on the fry pan and steam the chicken.
  6. Once both sides have browned, evaporate the excess water and add the stir fried vegetables and sauce. Once the sauce has coated the rest of the ingredients, the dish is done.
