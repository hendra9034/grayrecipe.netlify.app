The ingredients needed to make Minute Rice's Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Take 1 tbsp Cooking oil
  2. Take 1 lb Boneless, Skinless, Chicken or Pork pieces
  3. Make ready 16 oz Package frozen stir fry vegetables
  4. Prepare 8 oz Can, Undrained Pineapple chunks
  5. Make ready 10 1/2 oz Can low sodium chicken broth
  6. Get 2 cup Uncooked white rice
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup Sweet & Sour sauce
Instructions to make Minute Rice's Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Heat oil in a large skillet on medium heat. Add chicken or pork and cook and stir for 5 mins. Or until meat is well done.
  2. Add vegetables and pineapple w/juice. Mix well and stir in chicken broth. Bring to boil.
  3. Stir in rice and cover. Remove from heat when rice is tender and let stand for 5 mins. Stir in sweet and sour sauce.
