The ingredients needed to make My Go-To Dish Sweet & Sour Chicken for Chicken Lovers:
  1. Make ready 200 grams Chicken thigh meat
  2. Get For the marinade:
  3. Get 1 tbsp Sake
  4. Get 1/2 tsp Ginger (grated)
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Garlic (grated)
  6. Take 2/3 tbsp Soy sauce
  7. Prepare 1 Salt and pepper
  8. Take 1 Katakuriko
  9. Get 1/3 Carrot
  10. Prepare 1/2 Onion
  11. Take 3 Shitake mushrooms
  12. Get 50 grams Bamboo shoot (cooked and preserved in water)
  13. Get 2 Green peppers
  14. Prepare 1 Oil for cooking, frying
  15. Take For the sweet vinegar sauce:
  16. Get 3 tbsp Sugar
  17. Make ready 3 tbsp Rice vinegar
  18. Take 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  19. Take 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
  20. Take 1 tbsp Sake
  21. Get 1/2 tsp Chinese chicken stock powder
  22. Make ready 100 ml Water
  23. Take 1 tbsp Katakuriko
Steps to make My Go-To Dish Sweet & Sour Chicken for Chicken Lovers:
  1. Combine all of the ingredients for the sweet vinegar sauce.
  2. Add the sweet vinegar sauce. Mix well before marinating, since the katakuriko will settle. Mix everything together well.
  3. While marinating, cook the vegetables, starting with the root vegetables.
  4. Once the vegetables have cooked, add the fried chicken.
  5. Add the sweet vinegar sauce, mixing well before adding. Mix everything together well.
  6. It's ready to serve.
