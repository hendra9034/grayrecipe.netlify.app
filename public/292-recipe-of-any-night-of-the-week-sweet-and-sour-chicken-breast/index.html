Instructions to make Sweet and sour chicken breast:
  1. Wash chicken breast,marinate with curry,chicken cube and little oil and corn flour
  2. Fry in a hot oil until golden brown
  3. Strain in a strainer
  4. Crush onion and scotch bonnet and stir fry it with little oil,maggi,curry powder,brown sugar,honey,vinegar,lemon juice,curry powder and fried chicken breast
  5. Add in diced red and green paper
  6. Serve with rice or pasta
