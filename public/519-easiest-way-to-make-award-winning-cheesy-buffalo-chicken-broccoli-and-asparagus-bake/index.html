The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Buffalo Chicken Broccoli and Asparagus Bake:
  1. Make ready 1 lb diced chicken breast, cooked
  2. Get 1/2 cup hot sauce (any - I prefer Nando's)
  3. Make ready 12 oz alfredo sauce (store bought or make your own)
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup heavy cream
  5. Make ready 8 oz shredded cheese (any cheese is good here but gouda is best)
  6. Take 10 oz broccoli, steamed
  7. Get 10 oz asparagus, steamed
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup panko crumbs
  9. Take Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Cheesy Buffalo Chicken Broccoli and Asparagus Bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Spray a 9×13 casserole dish with cooking spray (olive oil is my preference)
  3. Toss together steamed broccoli and asparagus in the bottom of the casserole dish. (You can buy steam-in-the-bag for ease but I love to steam my vegetables in my instant pot).
  4. In a separate bowl, coat the chicken pieces in hot sauce. Transfer the chicken pieces in a layer on top of the broccoli and asparagus
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together alfredo sauce (will post my recipe later but you can use a jar of store bought) and heavy cream. Mix in about 3/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
  6. Pour alfredo sauce over the chicken and vegetables.
  7. Sprinkle the shredded cheese evenly over the top of the alfredo sauce. Finish with a dusting of panko crumbs
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and casserole is cooked through. Let stand 5 minutes before serving to allow for everything to set.
  9. Enjoy!
