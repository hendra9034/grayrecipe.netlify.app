The ingredients needed to make Chicken pasta and veggie bake:
  1. Take 1/2 Red capsicum diced
  2. Make ready 1/2 medium brown onion diced
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Frozen corn kernels
  4. Prepare 1 can 420g condenced cream of chicken soup
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter
  6. Make ready 2 1/2 cup Cooked spiral or penne pasta
  7. Make ready 2 1/2 cup Cooked chicken breast cubed
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup Water
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk
  10. Make ready 2 cup Cheddar cheese grated then halved
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup Breadcrumbs
  12. Make ready 1 To taste salt and pepper
  13. Take 1 To taste ground paprika

Steps to make Chicken pasta and veggie bake:
  1. Preheat oven 190 c
  2. In lge pot over med to high heat,saute onion and capsicum in butter until soft,add frozen corn,cook for 1 min
  3. Add soup,water and half the grated cheese,combine,add milk gradually while stirring
  4. Cook over med heat until cheese has melted,stir in pasta and chicken,season to taste with salt and pepper
  5. Transfer mixture into a greased casserole dish(approx 30x15cm). Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and paprika to taste
  6. Bake in oven for approx 15 mins,remove and top with remaining cheese, bake for a further 5 mins or until cheese has melted.
  7. Let stand for 3-6 mins,serve
