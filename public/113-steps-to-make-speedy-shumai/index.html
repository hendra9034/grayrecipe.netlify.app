Steps to make Shumai:
  1. Add chicken and all ingredients into the food processor. Blend until smooth paste then transfer to bowl.
  2. Add half shredded carrot and spring onion, mix well.
  3. Grab a wonton wrapper, add 2 tbsp filling and wrap it. Add some shredded carrot on top and steam it about 15-20 minutes.
  4. For the dipping sauce, mince garlic, bird's eye Chili and shallot. Saute until fragnant and add some water. Add chili sauce, rice vinegar, salt, and chicken stock powder. Bring to a boil. Shumai is ready to serve.Enjoy.
