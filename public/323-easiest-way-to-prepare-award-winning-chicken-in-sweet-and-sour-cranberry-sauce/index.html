The ingredients needed to make Chicken in Sweet and Sour Cranberry Sauce:
  1. Take 1 each of drumstick and wing or optional
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp oil
  3. Get SWEET AND SOUR CRANBERRY SAUCE
  4. Get 1 tbsp sherry wine vineger
  5. Make ready 1 cup dried cranberry
  6. Take 1 cup heinz tomato sauce
  7. Make ready 1 cup heinz chili sauce
  8. Take 2 tbsp light soy sauce
  9. Take 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  10. Make ready 2 tsp brown sugar
Instructions to make Chicken in Sweet and Sour Cranberry Sauce:
  1. PAN FRY CHICKEN
  2. with oil pan grill chicken till lightly brown for about 5 minute both side then drain
  3. BAKE IT
  4. put chicken with sweet and sour cranberry sauce at 200 C for 20 minute lightly cover top with foil
  5. baste with sauce then turn chicken over and bake further another 10 minute cover top
  6. lastly uncover and baste with sauce and bake for another 10 minute then off heat and serve
