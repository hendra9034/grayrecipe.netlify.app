The ingredients needed to make Chicken and Chorizo Bake:
  1. Prepare 2 Chicken Breasts
  2. Get 65 g Diced Chorizo
  3. Make ready 200 g Canned Spinach
  4. Take 125 g Courgette Spaghetti
  5. Prepare 150 g Butternut Squash
  6. Prepare 150 g Sweet Potato
  7. Get 200 ml Chicken Stock
  8. Get 1 tsp Garlic Puree
  9. Get Olive Oil
  10. Make ready Salt
  11. Prepare Black Pepper
  12. Take Paprika

Instructions to make Chicken and Chorizo Bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 200C. Cut chicken, butternut squash and sweet potato into large chunks. Place in casserole dish and drizzle with oil.
  2. Prepare chicken stock and mix in garlic Puree, and salt and pepper to taste. Pour mixture over the chicken.
  3. Place in the oven and cook for 25mins.
  4. Stir, then add chorizo, courgette spaghetti, spinach and paprika, and cook for a further 10mins.
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
