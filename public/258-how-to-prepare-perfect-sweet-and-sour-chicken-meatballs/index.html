Steps to make Sweet and sour chicken meatballs:
  1. In chopper add chicken breast along with salt,black and white pepper,red chilli powder..
  2. Now add egg and soya sauce..
  3. Then flour and corn flour..
  4. Chopperize well..
  5. Refrigerate for 30min..
  6. Now grease hand with oil..and make balls.. make around 12 to 14..
  7. Now in a pan heat oil..
  8. Now shallow fry all balls.. try to cover all four side..
  9. Remove and set a side..
  10. For sauce.. chopped garlic first
  11. Cubes cut onion and capsicum..
  12. In a wok heat oil with 1 tbsp chilli oil(recipe available in my list)..
  13. Add Chopped garlic and fry for a min..
  14. Now add onion and capsicum..
  15. Along with ketchup,sweet chilli sauce,soya sauve,chilli sauce and vinegar..
  16. Now add water along with chilli flakes and chicken powder cook for 5 min..
  17. Mix well.. in a bowl.diasolbe corn flour with water..
  18. When sauce start bubbling add corn flour and cook for 2,3min..
  19. Now add meatballs cook for 2,3min and remove from heat.
  20. Serve with egg fried rice.. Enjoyyy
