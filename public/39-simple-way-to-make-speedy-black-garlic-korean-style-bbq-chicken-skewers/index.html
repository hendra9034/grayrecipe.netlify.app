The ingredients needed to make Black Garlic Korean Style BBQ Chicken Skewers:
  1. Get 4 large metal (or bamboo) skewers
  2. Make ready Chicken thighs - 8 bone out, skin off, then sliced into pieces (big enough to fit on two skewers - but thin enough to cook fairly quickly)

Instructions to make Black Garlic Korean Style BBQ Chicken Skewers:
  1. Add the chopped chicken thighs to the buttermilk and coat thoroughly. Leave in the fridge preferably overnight or at least 3 hours.
  2. Add the sauce ingredients together in a bowl and mix together - set the bowl aside. (Take out approximately 5 tablespoons and also set aside in a separate bowl for dipping the cooked chicken.)
  3. Thread the buttermilk marinated chicken on to two skewers so it won’t rotate when you flip it.
  4. Brush with a little oil, then cook the plain skewers 6-8 minutes per side on the bbq (we always use a @barbequick) or under the grill til almost cooked through.
