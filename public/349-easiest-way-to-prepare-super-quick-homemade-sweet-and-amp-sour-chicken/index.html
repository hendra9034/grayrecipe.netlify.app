The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Make ready Chicken half kg
  2. Take 1 tsp Crushed black pepper
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Chilli sauce
  4. Take 1 tbsp Sirka
  5. Take 1 tbsp Crushed red chilli
  6. Take 1 tsp Mustard powder
  7. Get 2 tbsp Soya sauce
  8. Get 1 tbsp Lehsan paste
  9. Make ready 4 tbsp Corn flour
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp Pissa dhanya
  11. Take to taste Salt
  12. Make ready 1 cup Ketchup
  13. Get 1 tsp Garam masala powder
  14. Get 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
Instructions to make Sweet & Sour Chicken:
  1. Subse phele ketchup ke ilawa sub ingredients ko chicken pe laga ke marinate kar ke rakh dein.
  2. Phir oil garam karen r fry kar lein crispy se.
  3. Ab ek pan me ketcup 1 cup 1 tsp soya and chilli sauce 1 tsp vinegar add karen half cup pani dal ke 2 tbsp brown sugar add karen r stove pe thora thick kar lein.
