The ingredients needed to make Chicken Bake:
  1. Make ready 2 large chicken breasts
  2. Prepare 2 can cream of chicken soup (I use fat free)
  3. Make ready 1 can cream of mushroom soup (I use fat free)
  4. Take 1 packages frozen crescent rolls
  5. Take 1 small bag frozen carrots
  6. Get 1 small bag frozen corn
  7. Get 1 small bag frozen peas
  8. Take 1 small 8 oz or less bag of egg noodles
  9. Make ready 1 cup fat free sour cream
  10. Take 1 1/2 tsp cracked black pepper
Instructions to make Chicken Bake:
  1. preheat oven to 400
  2. takes 10-15 minutes to prep. 50 minutes to bake
  3. slice raw chicken breasts into small bite sized pieces
  4. spray medium rectangle casserole pan (10x14 or so) with cooking spray
  5. mix vegetables, chicken, soup, pepper together in casserole dish
  6. pull apart crescent rolls into small pieces and stir into mixture
  7. gradually stir in egg noodles. not necessary to use whole bag as could dry out casserole so eyeball a decent amount as you stir in
  8. Bake for 50 minutes
