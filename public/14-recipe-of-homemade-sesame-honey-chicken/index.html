The ingredients needed to make Sesame Honey Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 Chicken Breast’s
  2. Take 1 1/2 tablespoon honey
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  4. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon chilli flakes
  5. Make ready Dash soy sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon spring onion
  7. Get 1 tablespoon corn flour
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic
  9. Get Pinch salt
  10. Take 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
Steps to make Sesame Honey Chicken:
  1. Cut chicken breast into strips. Mid corn Flour,salt,garlic and black pepper. Coat chicken with this mixture.
  2. Deep fry chicken until golden brown on medium heat.
  3. In a saucepan add honey,sesame, chilli flakes. Once heated toss the chicken in and stir until covered in the mixture add in a dash of soy garnish with spring onion…
