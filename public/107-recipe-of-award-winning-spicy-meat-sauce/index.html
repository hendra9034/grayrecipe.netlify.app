The ingredients needed to make Spicy Meat Sauce:
  1. Make ready Vegetable Oil
  2. Make ready small piece Ginger *finely chopped OR grated
  3. Make ready Garlic *finely chopped
  4. Take Mince Meat *Pork, Beef, Veal, Chicken, etc
  5. Get Spring Onions *finely chopped
  6. Get Potato Starch OR Corn Starch *mixed with 1 teaspoon Water
  7. Prepare Sauce
  8. Make ready Dark Colour Miso
  9. Take Sugar
  10. Make ready Soy Sauce
  11. Prepare Sake (Rice wine)
  12. Make ready Toban Djan (Chilli Bean Sauce)
  13. Get Chicken Stock *OR 1/4 cup Water & 1/4 teaspoon Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  14. Take Sesame Oil *You can add some Chilli Oil as well
Steps to make Spicy Meat Sauce:
  1. Combine all the sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Heat Oil in a saucepan over medium heat, add Ginger and Garlic. When aromatic, add Mince Meat and Spring Onion.
  3. When Minced Meat is cooked, add the sauce. Cook for a few minutes, then add Potato Starch (OR Corn Starch) mixture and stir until thickens.
  4. Enjoy with Tofu, steamed Vegetables, Noodles or Rice. Today I served steamed Cauliflower with this sauce.
