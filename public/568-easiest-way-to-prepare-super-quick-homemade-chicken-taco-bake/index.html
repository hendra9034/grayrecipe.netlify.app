The ingredients needed to make Chicken Taco Bake:
  1. Make ready 3 chicken breasts (boiled & shredded)
  2. Take 1 can nacho cheese
  3. Take 2 cups tomato juice
  4. Prepare 1 tsp chili powder
  5. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Get 1 can chopped chilis
  7. Prepare 1 can diced tomatos with green chilis
  8. Make ready 5-6 flour tortillas
  9. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
  10. Prepare 1/2 onion (minced)
  11. Prepare 1 cup shredded fiesta cheese
  12. Take 1 can black olives
  13. Take Sour cream
  14. Get 2 roma tomatos (diced)
