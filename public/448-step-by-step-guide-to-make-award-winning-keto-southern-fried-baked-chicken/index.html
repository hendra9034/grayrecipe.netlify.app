The ingredients needed to make Keto Southern Fried (Baked :) ) Chicken:
  1. Prepare 2 Chicken Breasts
  2. Make ready 1 Large Egg
  3. Prepare 75 g Almond Flour
  4. Make ready 1.5 Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
  5. Take 1.5 Teaspoon Garlic Powder
  6. Get 1.5 Teaspoon Dried Mixed Herbs
  7. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon Sea Salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon Black Pepper
Steps to make Keto Southern Fried (Baked :) ) Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 200 and Grease a baking tray.
  2. Mix all of your spices and seasoning together in a mixing bowl.
  3. Whisk egg in a separate bowl.
  4. Slice the two chicken breasts horizontally/butterfly to create 4 steaks.
  5. Sprinkle one half of your spice mix onto the chicken, lightly coating both sides.
  6. Mix the remaining spice mix with the almond flour.
  7. Dip 1 steak into the whisked egg and then into the spiced almond flour mixture. Coat both sides well and place onto a greased baking sheet. Repeat with all chicken steaks.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes, carefully turning half way through cooking.
  9. I like mine served with a large salad and creamy broccoli salad or keto coleslaw. And of course - an extra dash of mayo :)
