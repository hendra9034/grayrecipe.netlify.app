The ingredients needed to make Loaded Baked Potato & Chicken Casserole:
  1. Make ready 2 lb Chicken breast, cubed
  2. Get 8 Potatoes, cubed
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup Olive oil
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp pepper
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp Garlic powder
  7. Get 6 tbsp Hot sauce , optional
  8. Make ready 2 cup Cheddar cheese, shredded
  9. Get 1 cup Bacon, crumbled
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup Green onion, diced
Instructions to make Loaded Baked Potato & Chicken Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 500°F
  2. In large bowl mix olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and hot sauce
  3. Add potatoes to bowl
  4. Coat 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray
  5. Add potatoes, leave excess oil in the bowl
  6. Bake potatoes for 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes
  7. I like to cook my chicken a little before I put it in the bowl with oil. Do not cook all the way
  8. Add cubed chicken to bowl with left over oil. allow to marinate while potatoes bake
  9. Ounce potatoes are done, add chicken
  10. Then bacon
  11. Bake for 15 more minutes.
  12. Then add cheese and green onion. cook for 3 more minutes
