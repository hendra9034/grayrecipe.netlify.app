The ingredients needed to make Chicken ‘Okowa’ Glutinous Rice:
  1. Prepare cup) Glutinous Rice *short grain preferred
  2. Take cup) Japanese Short Grain Rice
  3. Take Chicken Thigh Fillets *about 250g
  4. Take Sake
  5. Take Salt
  6. Take Bamboo Shoots (I use canned cooked Bamboo Shoots.) *sliced
  7. Take Soy Sauce
  8. Prepare Carrot *about 80g
  9. Get Shiitake *about 80g
  10. Make ready Toasted Sesame Seeds for topping
  11. Take Spring Onion for topping *finely sliced
  12. Make ready Soup
  13. Take Chicken or Dashi Stock *OR 2 cups Water & 2 teaspoons Asian Chicken Bouillon Powder
  14. Make ready Soy Sauce
  15. Get Mirin
Instructions to make Chicken ‘Okowa’ Glutinous Rice:
  1. Wash Glutinous Rice and Regular Short Grain Rice together and drain.
  2. Cut Chicken into 2cm size and place in a bowl. Add Sake and a pinch of Salt and massage, set aside.
  3. Place drained Bamboo Shoots in a bowl, add Soy Sauce and marinate while you are preparing other vegetables.
  4. Cut Carrot in half lengthways and slice 3 to 4 mm thick. Slice Shiitake. If you use dried Shiitake, they must be well rehydrated.
  5. Make soup by mixing all the ingredients.
  6. Place Chicken pieces in the rice cooker’s inner pot, cover with well-drained Rice, place Bamboo Shoots, Carrot and Shiitake slices over the Rice evenly. DO NOT MIX. Gently pour the soup.
  7. Press the Cook/Start button. When the rice is cooked and steamed, open the lid and toss the rice gently.
  8. Sprinkle with some Toasted Sesame Seeds and chopped Spring Onions and serve.
