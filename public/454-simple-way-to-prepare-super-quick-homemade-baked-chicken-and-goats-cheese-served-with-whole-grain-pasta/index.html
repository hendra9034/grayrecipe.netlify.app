The ingredients needed to make Baked Chicken and Goats Cheese served with whole grain pasta:
  1. Make ready 2 chicken breasts
  2. Get 120 g goats cheese
  3. Take 1 can tinned tomatoes
  4. Make ready 200 g dried pasta
  5. Prepare Olive oil
  6. Prepare Salt & pepper
Steps to make Baked Chicken and Goats Cheese served with whole grain pasta:
  1. Preheat oven to 180c (fan oven). Put chicken breasts in a roasting dish. Cover chicken in tinned tomatoes and sliced goats cheese.
  2. Bake chicken for 25 to 30 minutes. Boil pasta as per packet instructions. After cooking time slice the chicken to check it is cooked throughout.
  3. Plate up with pasta first then carefully add goats cheese coated chicken. Drizzle a little olive oil and season with salt & pepper.
