##### Instructions to make Parmesan herb chicken:

1. Heat a heavy skillet with vegetable oil and add the garlic until it turns golden brown
1. Add the chicken with the marinade and sauté until both sides turn golden brown
1. Dissolve the bouillon in some water and add into the chicken
1. Cook the chicken until its cooked through, then add the butter and stir until butter melts and mixes with the chicken. Then turn heat off.
1. Add chopped scallions, parsley and Parmesan cheese and stir to mix well and until cheese is melted
1. Set aside and serve over a salad, in a sandwich or a wrap!
